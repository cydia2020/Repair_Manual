Title: LIGHTING (EXT): REAR COMBINATION LIGHT ASSEMBLY: DISASSEMBLY; 2019 - 2022 MY Prius [12/2018 -        ]

DISASSEMBLY

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

  • Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.
  • The following procedure is for the LH side.

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE BACK-UP LIGHT BULB

(a) Turn the rear combination light socket and wire sub-assembly with the back-up light bulb as shown in the illustration to disconnect them as a unit.

Remove in this Direction

(b) Remove the back-up light bulb from the rear combination light socket and wire sub-assembly.

2. REMOVE REAR TURN SIGNAL LIGHT BULB

(a) Turn the rear combination light socket and wire sub-assembly with the rear turn signal light bulb as shown in the illustration to disconnect them as a unit.

Remove in this Direction

(b) Remove the rear turn signal light bulb from the rear combination light socket and wire sub-assembly.

3. REMOVE REAR SIDE MARKER LIGHT BULB

(a) Turn the rear combination light socket and wire sub-assembly with the rear side marker light bulb as shown in the illustration to disconnect them as a unit.

Remove in this Direction

(b) Remove the rear side marker light bulb from the rear combination light socket and wire sub-assembly.

4. REMOVE REAR COMBINATION LIGHT SOCKET AND WIRE SUB-ASSEMBLY

(a) Disengage the clamp to remove the rear combination light socket and wire sub-assembly.

5. REMOVE REAR COMBINATION LIGHT LENS AND BODY

6. REMOVE TAIL LIGHT LED

NOTICE:

If the tail light LED has been removed, replace the tail light LED gasket with a new one.

(a) Using a T20 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the screw.

Remove in this Direction

(b) Turn the tail light LED as shown in the illustration to remove it.

7. REMOVE STOP LIGHT LED

NOTICE:

If the stop light LED has been removed, replace the stop light LED gasket with a new one.

(a) Using a T20 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the screw.

Remove in this Direction

(b) Turn the stop light LED as shown in the illustration to remove it.

8. REMOVE TAIL LIGHT LED GASKET

NOTICE:

The tail light LED gasket cannot be reused.

(a) Remove the tail light LED gasket.

9. REMOVE STOP LIGHT LED GASKET

NOTICE:

The stop light LED gasket cannot be reused.

(a) Remove the stop light LED gasket.

10. REMOVE NO. 2 REAR COMBINATION LIGHT LENS AND BODY
