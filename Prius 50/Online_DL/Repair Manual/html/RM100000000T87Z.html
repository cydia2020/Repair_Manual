Title: HEATING / AIR CONDITIONING: FRONT BLOWER MOTOR: REMOVAL; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

REMOVAL

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE NO. 2 INSTRUMENT PANEL UNDER COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY

Click here

2. REMOVE BLOWER MOTOR WITH FAN SUB-ASSEMBLY

(a) Disengage the clamp.

(b) Disconnect the connector.

(c) Remove the 3 screws and blower motor with fan sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

Replace the blower motor with fan sub-assembly if it has been dropped or subjected to a severe impact.
