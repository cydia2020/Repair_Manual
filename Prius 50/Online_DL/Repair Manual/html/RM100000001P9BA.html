Title: CELLULAR COMMUNICATION: SAFETY CONNECT SYSTEM (for 7 Inch Display): B15C111,B15C113; GNSS Antenna Circuit Short to Ground; 2021 - 2022 MY Prius [07/2020 -        ]

DTC

B15C111

GNSS Antenna Circuit Short to Ground

DTC

B15C113

GNSS Antenna Circuit Open

DESCRIPTION

These DTCs are stored when a malfunction occurs in the telephone and GPS antenna (for Roof Side) circuit.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

B15C111

GNSS Antenna Circuit Short to Ground

Current to the GNSS antenna is lower than the malfunction threshold for 10 seconds or more when the power switch is on (IG)

(Short circuit)

  • DCM (telematics transceiver)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna (for Roof Side)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (antenna cord sub-assembly (for TypeB))
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (antenna cord sub-assembly (for Type C))
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (No. 2 antenna cord sub-assembly)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (No. 3 antenna cord sub-assembly)

B15C113

GNSS Antenna Circuit Open

Current to the GNSS antenna is higher than the malfunction threshold for 10 seconds or more when the power switch is on (IG)

(Open circuit)

  • DCM (telematics transceiver)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna (for Roof Side)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (antenna cord sub-assembly (for TypeB))
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (antenna cord sub-assembly (for Type C))
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (No. 2 antenna cord sub-assembly)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (No. 3 antenna cord sub-assembly)

PROCEDURE

1.

CHECK DTC

(a) Turn the power switch off.

(b) Connect the Techstream to the DLC3.

(c) Turn the power switch on (IG) and wait for 10 seconds or more.

(d) Turn the Techstream on.

(e) Clear the DTCs.

Body Electrical > Telematics > Clear DTCs

(f) Check for DTCs and check that no DTCs are output.

Body Electrical > Telematics > Trouble Codes

OK:

No DTCs are output.
