Engine Oil Level Sensor Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The combination meter assembly (meter circuit plate) and engine oil level sensor are connected via direct line. The combination meter assembly (meter circuit plate) determines the engine oil level based on the engine oil level sensor status signal.

HINT:

The coolant temperature signal and engine speed signal is sent from the ECM to the combination meter assembly (meter circuit plate) via CAN communication. Because an accurate engine oil level cannot be detected when the engine is cold or operating at high speeds, warning control is only performed after the engine is warmed up and the engine is stopped or idling.
