Title: LIGHTING (INT): LIGHTING SYSTEM: Front Door Courtesy Switch Circuit; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

Front Door Courtesy Switch Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) detects the condition of the front door courtesy light switch assembly.

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

Before replacing the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU), refer to Registration.
