NOTICE:

  • In order to reduce the risk of damage to the part and to ensure ease of operations, secure a space of 80 mm (3.15 in.) or more as shown in the illustration.

    *a

    80 mm (3.15 in.)

  • Secure enough space to ensure that the upper seat track rail sub-assembly LH does not contact any other objects during installation.

(a) Disconnect the slide motor connector.

(b) Disengage the claw and 2 clamps.

(c) Remove the 2 screws.

(d) Using a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape, disengage the 2 claws.

Remove in this direction

Protective Tape

(e) Disengage the guide to remove the front seat cushion wire harness holder LH as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

When remove the front seat cushion wire harness holder LH, take care not to damage each connector and wire harness.

(f) Using a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape, disengage the claw.

Remove in this direction

Protective Tape

(g) Disengage the guide to remove the front seat lower cushion shield LH from the upper seat track rail sub-assembly LH as shown in the illustration.

(h) Using a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape, disengage the claw.
