2. REMOVE DIFFERENTIAL SIDE GEAR SHAFT SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:

Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.

3. REMOVE DIFFERENTIAL SIDE GEAR SHAFT LH TYPE T OIL SEAL

(a) Using SST, remove the differential side gear shaft LH type T oil seal from the rear traction motor with transaxle assembly.

SST: 09308-00010

NOTICE:

Do not damage the rear traction motor with transaxle assembly.

4. REMOVE DIFFERENTIAL SIDE GEAR SHAFT RH TYPE T OIL SEAL

(a) Using SST, remove the differential side gear shaft RH type T oil seal from the rear traction motor with transaxle assembly.

SST: 09612-30012

NOTICE:

Do not damage the rear traction motor with transaxle assembly.

*b

Turn

5. INSTALL DIFFERENTIAL SIDE GEAR SHAFT LH TYPE T OIL SEAL

(a) Using SST and a hammer, install a new differential side gear shaft LH type T oil seal to the rear traction motor with transaxle assembly.

SST: 09316-10010

SST: 09950-70010

09951-07100

Standard Depth:

-0.5 to 0.5 mm (-0.0197 to 0.0197 in.)

NOTICE:

  • Evenly tap in the differential side gear shaft LH type T oil seal to prevent it from being installed at an angle.
  • Be careful not to damage the lip of the differential side gear shaft LH type T oil seal.

*a

Depth

(b) Coat the lip of the differential side gear shaft LH type T oil seal with a small amount of MP grease.

6. INSTALL DIFFERENTIAL SIDE GEAR SHAFT RH TYPE T OIL SEAL

(a) Using SST and a hammer, install a new differential side gear shaft RH type T oil seal to the rear traction motor with transaxle assembly.

SST: 09316-60012

09316-00011

Standard Depth:

-0.5 to 0.5 mm (-0.0197 to 0.0197 in.)

NOTICE:

  • Evenly tap in the differential side gear shaft RH type T oil seal to prevent it from being installed at an angle.
  • Be careful not to damage the lip of the differential side gear shaft RH type T oil seal.

*a

Depth

(b) Coat the lip of the differential side gear shaft RH type T oil seal with a small amount of MP grease.
