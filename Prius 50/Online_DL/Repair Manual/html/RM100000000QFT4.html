Title: AUDIO / VIDEO: AUDIO AND VISUAL SYSTEM: B15C3; Speaker Output Short; 2016 - 2019 MY Prius [11/2015 - 08/2019]

DTC

B15C3

Speaker Output Short

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when a malfunction occurs in the speakers.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

B15C3

Speaker Output Short

A short is detected in the speaker output circuit

  • Harness or connector
  • Speaker
  • Radio and display receiver assembly

WIRING DIAGRAM

PROCEDURE

1.

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (RADIO AND DISPLAY RECEIVER ASSEMBLY - BODY GROUND)

(a) Disconnect the F32 and F33 radio and display receiver assembly connectors.

(b) Disconnect the G3 and G6 front No. 2 speaker assembly connectors.

(c) Disconnect the K4 and L5 rear speaker assembly connectors.

(d) Measure the resistance between the radio and display receiver assembly and body ground to check for a short circuit in the wire harness.
