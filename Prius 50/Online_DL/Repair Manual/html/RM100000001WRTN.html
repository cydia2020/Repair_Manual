Headlight Dimmer Switch Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) receives the following switch information:

  • Light control switch in off*1, DRL OFF*2, tail, head, AUTO*4 or DRL*5 position
  • Dimmer switch in high, low or high flash (pass) position
  • Fog light switch in front or off position*3
  • *1: w/ OFF Switch
  • *2: w/ DRL OFF Switch
  • *3: w/ Front Fog Light
  • *4: w/ Automatic Light Control
  • *5: w/o Automatic Light Control
