Title: SEAT: FRONT SEAT ASSEMBLY (for Power Seat): INSPECTION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT FRONT SEAT ADJUSTER ASSEMBLY LH

(a) Check the operation of the slide motor.

(1) Check that the seat cushion moves smoothly when the auxiliary battery is connected to the slide motor connector terminals.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(b1)
Click Connector(b1)

Tester Connection

Operational Direction

b1-2 - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

b1-1 - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Seat cushion moves forward

b1-1 - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

b1-2 - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Seat cushion moves backward

If the result is not as specified, replace the upper seat track rail sub-assembly LH.

*a

Component without harness connected

(Slide Motor (Front Seat Adjuster Assembly LH)

(b) Check the operation of the front vertical motor.

(1) Check that the seat cushion moves smoothly when the auxiliary battery is connected to the front vertical motor connector terminals.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(b2)
Click Connector(b2)

Tester Connection

Operational Direction

b2-2 - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

b2-1 - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Seat cushion moves upward

b2-1 - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

b2-2 - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Seat cushion moves downward

If the result is not as specified, replace the front seat adjuster assembly LH.

*a

Component without harness connected

(Front Vertical Motor (Front Seat Adjuster Assembly LH)

(c) Check the operation of the lifter motor.

(1) Check that the seat cushion moves smoothly when the auxiliary battery is connected to the lifter motor connector terminals.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(b3)
Click Connector(b3)

Tester Connection

Operational Direction

b3-2 - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

b3-1 - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Seat cushion moves upward

b3-1 - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

b3-2 - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Seat cushion moves downward

If the result is not as specified, replace the front seat adjuster assembly LH.

*a

Component without harness connected

(Lifter Motor (Front Seat Adjuster Assembly LH)

2. INSPECT FRONT SEAT BACK FRAME SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

(a) Check the operation of the reclining motor.

(1) Check that the seatback moves smoothly when the auxiliary battery is connected to the reclining motor connector terminals.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(d1)
Click Connector(d1)

Tester Connection

Operational Direction

If the result is not as specified, replace the front seatback frame sub-assembly LH.
