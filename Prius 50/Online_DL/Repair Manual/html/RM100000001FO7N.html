Title: Q510 (HYBRID TRANSMISSION / TRANSAXLE): MOTOR CABLE: INSTALLATION; 2019 - 2022 MY Prius [12/2018 -        ]

INSTALLATION

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL REAR TRACTION MOTOR CABLE

CAUTION:

Be sure to wear insulated gloves.

(a) Using an insulated tool, install the rear traction motor cable to the rear traction motor with transaxle assembly with the 3 bolts.

Torque:

10 N·m {102 kgf·cm, 7 ft·lbf}

NOTICE:

  • Keep the rear traction motor cable terminals and their installation area free from foreign matter, water, etc.
  • Do not touch the waterproof seal or terminals of the rear traction motor cable.

(b) Engage the 2 claws to install a new motor cable terminal cap to the rear traction motor with transaxle assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Keep the motor cable terminal cap and its installation area free from foreign matter, water, etc.
  • Do not pinch the waterproof seal.
  • Check that the motor cable terminal cap is securely installed.

(c) Slide the connector cover as shown in the illustration and temporarily install the rear traction motor cable to the rear traction motor with transaxle assembly with the 3 bolts.

(d) Fully tighten the 3 bolts.

Torque:

10 N·m {102 kgf·cm, 7 ft·lbf}

(e) Engage the 3 clamps.

6. CONNECT REAR TRACTION MOTOR CABLE

CAUTION:

Be sure to wear insulated gloves.

NOTICE:

Do not allow any foreign matter or water to enter the rear hybrid motor control inverter assembly.

(a) Insert the rear traction motor cable into the cabin and install the grommet of the rear traction motor cable to the vehicle body.

*a

Positioning Mark

Rear of Vehicle

NOTICE:

Make sure that the arrow of the grommet is positioned between the positioning marks as shown in the illustration.

(b) Engage the clamp.

(c) Using an insulated tool, temporarily install the rear traction motor cable to the rear hybrid motor control inverter assembly with the 5 bolts.

NOTICE:

  • To prevent the threads from being damaged, temporarily tighten the 5 bolts by hand.
  • Do not touch the waterproof seal or terminals of the rear traction motor cable.
  • Do not damage the terminals, connector housing or rear hybrid motor control inverter assembly during connection.

*a

Waterproof Seal

(d) Using an insulated tool, fully tighten the 2 bolts (A).

Torque:

8.0 N·m {82 kgf·cm, 71 in·lbf}

(e) Using an insulated tool, fully tighten the 3 bolts (B).

Torque:

8.0 N·m {82 kgf·cm, 71 in·lbf}
