Title: CRUISE CONTROL: DYNAMIC RADAR CRUISE CONTROL SYSTEM: Cruise Control Switch Circuit; 2021 - 2022 MY Prius [07/2020 -        ]

Cruise Control Switch Circuit

DESCRIPTION

  • The cruise control main switch outputs the dynamic cruise control on/off signal and various control signals to the hybrid vehicle control ECU.
  • The hybrid vehicle control ECU controls the dynamic cruise control system according to the signals received from the cruise control main switch.
