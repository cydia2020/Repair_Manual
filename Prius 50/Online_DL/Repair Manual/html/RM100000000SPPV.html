Title: 2ZR-FXE (ENGINE CONTROL): CAMSHAFT POSITION SENSOR: REMOVAL; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

REMOVAL

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE NO. 1 ENGINE COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY

Click here

2. REMOVE CAMSHAFT POSITION SENSOR

(a) Disconnect the No. 1 fuel vapor feed hose from the fuel vapor feed pipe.

(b) Disconnect the camshaft position sensor connector.

(c) Remove the bolt and camshaft position sensor from the cylinder head cover sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

If a camshaft position sensor has been struck or dropped, replace it.
