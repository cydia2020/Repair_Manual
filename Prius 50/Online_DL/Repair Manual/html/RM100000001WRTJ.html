Headlight Dimmer Switch Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) receives the following switch information:

  • Light control switch in, DRL OFF*1, tail, head or AUTO position
  • Dimmer switch in high, low or high flash (pass) position
  • Fog light switch in front or off position*2
  • *1: w/ DRL OFF Switch
  • *2: w/ Front Fog Light
