Title: EXTERIOR PANELS / TRIM: BACK DOOR OUTSIDE GARNISH: INSTALLATION; 2016 - 2018 MY Prius [11/2015 - 12/2018]

INSTALLATION

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL BACK DOOR OUTSIDE GARNISH SUB-ASSEMBLY

(a) Pull back the lower No. 2 back window moulding and secure it with tape as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the lower No. 2 back window moulding.

HINT:

  • Use wide tape to ensure strong enough adhesion.
  • Use non-residue tape.
  • If necessary, clean the lower No. 2 back window moulding before securing it with tape.

*1

Lower No. 2 Back Window Moulding

*a

Tape

(b) Engage the 13 clips as shown in the illustration.

Install in this Direction

HINT:

Check that the lower No. 2 back window moulding is not caught under the back door outside garnish sub-assembly.

(c) Install the back door outside garnish sub-assembly with the 6 bolts.

Torque:

4.9 N·m {50 kgf·cm, 43 in·lbf}

(d) Remove the tape.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the lower No. 2 back window moulding.
