REPAIR

PROCEDURE

1. REPAIR WINDOW GLASS ANTENNA WIRE

*a

Repair Point

*b

Masking Tape

*c

Broken Wire

(a) Clean the broken wire tips with a grease, wax and silicone remover.

(b) Place masking tape along both sides of the wire to be repaired.

(c) Thoroughly mix the repair agent (Dupont paste No. 4817 or equivalent).

(d) Using a fine tip brush, apply a small amount of the repair agent to the wire.

*a

Fine Tip Brush

*b

Repair Point

(e) After a few minutes, remove the masking tape.
