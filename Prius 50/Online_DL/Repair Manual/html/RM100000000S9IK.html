Title: P610 (HYBRID TRANSMISSION / TRANSAXLE): SHIFT CONTROL ACTUATOR: INSTALLATION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSTALLATION

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL SHIFT CONTROL ACTUATOR ASSEMBLY

(a) Clean and degrease the bolt holes of the hybrid vehicle transaxle assembly.

(b) Apply adhesive to 2 or 3 threads on the ends of 2 new shift control actuator bolts.

Adhesive:

Toyota Genuine Adhesive 1324, Three Bond 1324 or equivalent

NOTICE:

Make sure to install the 2 shift control actuator bolts immediately after applying adhesive to prevent foreign matter from attaching to them.

*a

Adhesive

(c) Using a 12 mm hexagon socket wrench, temporarily install the shift control actuator assembly to the hybrid vehicle transaxle assembly with the 2 shift control actuator bolts.

NOTICE:

Do not tap the shift control actuator assembly with a plastic hammer or similar tool when installing it.

HINT:

As the position of the shift control actuator assembly is detected automatically, initialization is not necessary after disconnecting and reconnecting the cable to the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal.

(d) Apply adhesive to 2 or 3 threads on the ends of a new shift control actuator bolt.

Adhesive:

Toyota Genuine Adhesive 1324, Three Bond 1324 or equivalent

NOTICE:

Make sure to install the shift control actuator bolt immediately after applying adhesive to prevent foreign matter from attaching to it.

*a

Adhesive

(e) Temporarily install the shift control actuator bolt.

(f) Using a 12 mm hexagon socket wrench, tighten the 2 shift control actuator bolts.

Torque:

16.5 N·m {168 kgf·cm, 12 ft·lbf}

(g) Tighten the shift control actuator bolt.

Torque:

16.5 N·m {168 kgf·cm, 12 ft·lbf}

(h) Apply adhesive to 2 or 3 threads on the ends of 2 new bolts.

Adhesive:

Toyota Genuine Adhesive 1324, Three Bond 1324 or equivalent

NOTICE:

Make sure to install the 2 bolts immediately after applying adhesive to prevent foreign matter from attaching to them.

*a

Adhesive

(i) Install the 2 bolts to the 2 shift control actuator bolts.

*a

Bolt Head Breaking Position

-

-

NOTICE:

Tighten the bolts until the bolt heads break off.

HINT:

Breaking Torque: 4.9 to 8.8 N*m (50 to 90 kgf*cm, 43 to 78 in.*lbf)

(j) Connect the shift control actuator assembly connector.

2. INSTALL NO. 1 ENGINE UNDER COVER
