Title: TIRE PRESSURE MONITORING: TIRE PRESSURE WARNING SYSTEM: C2126; Transmitter ID not Received (Main); 2019 - 2021 MY Prius [12/2018 - 06/2021]

DTC

C2126

Transmitter ID not Received (Main)

DESCRIPTION

If the tire pressure warning ECU and receiver does not receive data from the tire pressure warning valve and transmitters after the tire pressure warning valve and transmitter ID registration is completed using the Techstream, the tire pressure warning ECU and receiver stores DTC C2126.

After ID registration is complete using the Techstream, the tire pressure warning light illuminates after blinking for 1 minute. When the tire pressure warning ECU and receiver receives the data from all tire pressure warning valve and transmitters with registered IDs, the tire pressure is judged to be normal and the tire pressure warning light turns off.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

Note

C2126

Transmitter ID not Received (Main)

After transmitter ID registration is completed, ECU does not receive data in 1 trip from transmitters whose IDs are stored in ECU.

  • Transmitter ID registration failure
  • Tire pressure warning valve and transmitter
  • Tire pressure warning ECU and receiver

-

NOTICE:

ID Switching Function:

Even when the 2nd tire set is selected, "(Main)" is displayed on the Techstream.

HINT:

The purpose of this DTC is to help prevent delivering a vehicle that has incorrectly registered transmitter IDs. After all IDs are registered, DTC C2126 is detected and the tire pressure warning light blinks for 1 minute and then illuminates. If the tire pressure warning light does not go off after a little while, the transmitter IDs may be incorrectly registered.

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

  • When replacing the tire pressure warning ECU and receiver or tire pressure warning valve and transmitter, read the transmitter IDs and number of the transmitters (4 or 5) stored in the old ECU using the Techstream and write them down before removal.
  • It is necessary to perform initialization after registration of the transmitter IDs into the tire pressure warning ECU and receiver if the ECU and/or one of the valve and transmitters has been replaced.
