Title: MIRROR (EXT): OUTER REAR VIEW MIRROR GLASS: REMOVAL; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

REMOVAL

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

  • Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.
  • The following procedure is for the LH side.

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE OUTER MIRROR

(a) Apply protective tape to the areas shown in the illustration.

Protective Tape

(b) Push the upper part of the mirror surface and tilt it.

(c) Using a moulding remover, disengage the 2 claws on the lower part of the outer mirror as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

Do not push the outer mirror with excessive force. Doing so may cause the actuator to come off or break the mirror surface.

(d) Push the lower part of the mirror surface and tilt it.

(e) Using a moulding remover, disengage the 2 claws on the upper part of the outer mirror as shown in the illustration.

Protective Tape

NOTICE:

Do not push the outer mirror with excessive force. Doing so may cause the actuator to come off or break the mirror surface.

(f) w/o Blind Spot Monitor System:

(1) Disconnect the connector to remove the outer mirror.

(g) w/ Blind Spot Monitor System:

(1) Disconnect the connector.

(2) Disengage the claw as shown in the illustration.

Push in this Direction

(3) Disengage the clamp.

(4) Disconnect the connector to remove the outer mirror.
