Title: EXTERIOR PANELS / TRIM: QUARTER GARNISH: INSTALLATION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSTALLATION

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

  • Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.
  • The following procedure is for the LH side.

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL QUARTER PILLAR COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY

HINT:

When installing a new quarter pillar cover sub-assembly, heat the vehicle body and quarter pillar cover sub-assembly using a heat light.

CAUTION:

  • Do not touch the heat light and heated parts, touching the heat light may result in burns.
  • Touching heated parts for a long time may result in burns.

*a

Heated Part

*b

Heat Light

Heating Temperature

Item

Temperature

Vehicle Body

40 to 60°C (104 to 140°F)

Quarter Pillar Cover Sub-assembly

20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

NOTICE:

Do not heat the vehicle body or quarter pillar cover sub-assembly excessively.

(a) Clean the vehicle body surface.

(1) Using a heat light, heat the vehicle body surface.

(2) Remove any double-sided tape from the vehicle body.

(3) Wipe off any tape adhesive residue with cleaner.

(b) Remove the release paper from a new quarter pillar cover sub-assembly.

HINT:

After removing the release paper, keep the exposed adhesive free from foreign matter.

(c) Engage the 6 clips and attach the double-sided tape as shown in the illustration to install the quarter pillar cover sub-assembly.

*a

Double-sided Tape

Install in this Direction

HINT:

Press the quarter pillar cover sub-assembly to install it.
