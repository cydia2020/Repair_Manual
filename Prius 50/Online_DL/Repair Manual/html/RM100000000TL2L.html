Operating Light Control Rheostat does not Change Light Brightness

DESCRIPTION

The combination meter assembly (meter circuit plate) receives signals from this circuit to adjust the illumination of the combination meter assembly (meter circuit plate). The combination meter assembly (meter circuit plate) sets the illumination level based on the operation of the light control rheostat switch in the combination switch assembly.

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

  • The meter illumination level can be adjusted by pressing the light control rheostat switch.
  • The meter illumination dims when the taillights are turned on.
  • Setting the meter illumination level to maximum brightness cancels the above dimming of the meter illumination.
