Title: LIGHTING (INT): FOOTWELL LIGHT: INSPECTION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT NO. 1 INTERIOR ILLUMINATION LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH

(a) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the connector and check that the No. 1 interior illumination light LH comes on.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(F5)
Click Connector(F5)

Tester Connection

Condition

Specified Condition

F5-2 (B) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

F5-1 (L) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Always

No. 1 interior illumination light LH comes on

If the result is not as specified, replace the No. 1 interior illumination light assembly LH.

*a

Component without harness connected

(No. 1 Interior Illumination Light Assembly LH)

2. INSPECT NO. 1 INTERIOR ILLUMINATION LIGHT ASSEMBLY RH

(a) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the connector and check that the No. 1 interior illumination light RH comes on.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(F4)
Click Connector(F4)

Tester Connection

Condition

Specified Condition

F4-2 (B) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

F4-1 (L) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Always

No. 1 interior illumination light RH comes on

If the result is not as specified, replace the No. 1 interior illumination light assembly RH.

*a

Component without harness connected

(No. 1 Interior Illumination Light Assembly RH)
