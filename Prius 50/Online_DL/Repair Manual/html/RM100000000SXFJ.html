Title: PARK ASSIST / MONITORING: INTUITIVE PARKING ASSIST SYSTEM: C1AEC; Front Sensor Communication Malfunction; 2016 - 2018 MY Prius [11/2015 - 12/2018]

DTC

C1AEC

Front Sensor Communication Malfunction

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when there is an open or short circuit in the communication line between the front sensors and the ECU, or when there is a malfunction in a front sensor.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

C1AEC

Front Sensor Communication Malfunction

An open or short circuit in the communication line between the front sensors and ECU or a malfunction in a front sensor during initialization mode after the power switch is turned on (IG).

  • Front ultrasonic sensor circuit
  • Harness or connector
  • Clearance warning ECU assembly
