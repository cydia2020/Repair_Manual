Title: LIGHTING (EXT): AUTOMATIC HEADLIGHT BEAM LEVEL CONTROL SYSTEM: B2415; Vehicle Speed Sensor; 2019 - 2022 MY Prius [12/2018 -        ]

DTC

B2415

Vehicle Speed Sensor

DESCRIPTION

The No. 1 headlight ECU sub-assembly LH receives signals indicating the vehicle speed signal from the skid control ECU (brake booster with master cylinder assembly) using CAN communication. The No. 1 headlight ECU sub-assembly LH stores DTC B2415.

PROCEDURE

1.

CLEAR DTC

(a) Connect the Techstream to the DLC3.

(b) Turn the power switch on (IG).

(c) Turn the Techstream on.

(d) Enter the following menus: Body Electrical / AFS / Trouble Codes.

(e) Clear the DTCs.

Body Electrical > AFS > Clear DTCs
