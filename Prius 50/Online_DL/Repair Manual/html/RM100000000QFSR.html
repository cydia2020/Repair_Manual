Data Signal Circuit between Radio Receiver and Stereo Jack Adapter

DESCRIPTION

The No. 1 stereo jack adapter assembly sends the sound data signal or image data signal from a USB device to the radio and display receiver assembly via this circuit.

(a) Disconnect the F91 radio and display receiver assembly connector.

(b) Disconnect the F39 No. 1 stereo jack adapter assembly connector.

(c) Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.
