2. REMOVE FUEL PRESSURE REGULATOR ASSEMBLY

(a) Disengage the 2 claws and remove the fuel pressure regulator holder from the fuel suction tube with pump and gauge assembly.

(b) Using a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape, remove the fuel pressure regulator assembly from the fuel suction tube with pump and gauge assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Pull out the fuel pressure regulator assembly carefully because the O-rings are firmly installed between the fuel pressure regulator assembly and fuel suction tube with pump and gauge assembly.
  • Do not damage the fuel suction tube with pump and gauge assembly.
