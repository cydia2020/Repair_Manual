Title: POWER DISTRIBUTION: INTEGRATION RELAY: INSPECTION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT NO. 1 INTEGRATION RELAY

(a) IG1 NO. 3 RELAY:

(1) Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

Standard Resistance:

Tester Connection

Condition

Specified Condition

1A-4 - 1A-3

Auxiliary battery voltage not applied between terminals 1A-1 and 1A-2

10 kΩ or higher

1A-4 - 1A-3

Auxiliary battery positive (+) → 1A-1

Auxiliary battery negative (-) → 1A-2

Below 1 Ω

If the result is not as specified, replace the No. 1 integration relay.

*a

Component without harness connected

(No. 1 Integration Relay)
