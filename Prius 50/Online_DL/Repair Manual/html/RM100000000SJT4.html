Title: EXTERIOR PANELS / TRIM: BACK DOOR OUTSIDE GARNISH: DISASSEMBLY; 2016 - 2018 MY Prius [11/2015 - 12/2018]

DISASSEMBLY

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

When removing the name plates, heat the back door outside garnish and name plates using a heat light.

Heating Temperature

Item

Temperature

  • Back Door Outside Garnish
  • Name Plate

20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F)

CAUTION:

  • Do not touch the heat light and heated parts, touching the heat light may result in burns.
  • Touching heated parts for a long time may result in burns.

*a

Heated Part

*b

Heat Light

NOTICE:

Do not heat the back door outside garnish or name plate excessively.

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE NO. 2 BACK DOOR OUTSIDE GARNISH PROTECTOR

(a) Remove the No. 2 back door outside garnish protector.

HINT:

Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.

2. REMOVE BACK DOOR OUTSIDE GARNISH PROTECTOR

(a) Remove the back door outside garnish protector.

HINT:

Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.
