Title: CELLULAR COMMUNICATION: SAFETY CONNECT SYSTEM: B15C5; Manual Button Malfunction; 2018 - 2020 MY Prius [11/2017 - 07/2020]

DTC

B15C5

Manual Button Malfunction

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is set when the DCM (Telematics Transceiver) detects an open or short circuit in the manual (SOS) switch.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

B15C5

Manual Button Malfunction

Open or short circuit in manual (SOS) switch is detected for 10 seconds or more.

  • DCM (Telematics Transceiver)
  • Map light assembly
  • Wire harness or connector

w/ Sliding Roof
w/o Sliding Roof
