10. DISCONNECT DISCHARGE HOSE SUB-ASSEMBLY

(a) Remove the bolt and disconnect the discharge hose sub-assembly from the cooler condenser assembly.

(b) Remove the O-ring from the discharge hose sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

Seal the openings of the disconnected parts using vinyl tape to prevent entry of moisture and foreign matter.

11. DISCONNECT AIR CONDITIONER TUBE AND ACCESSORY ASSEMBLY

(a) While pressing the end of the air conditioner tube and accessory assembly into the end of the cooler condenser assembly, use pliers to squeeze together both sides of the piping clamp until it breaks apart.

(b) Disconnect the air conditioner tube and accessory assembly.

NOTICE:

NOTICE:

Wrap the open ends of the separated cooler condenser assembly and air conditioner tube and accessory assembly with vinyl tape to prevent entry of moisture and foreign matter.

(d) Remove the piping clamp.
