Title: P610 (HYBRID TRANSMISSION / TRANSAXLE): SHIFT CONTROL ACTUATOR: REMOVAL; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

REMOVAL

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE NO. 1 ENGINE UNDER COVER

Click here

2. REMOVE SHIFT CONTROL ACTUATOR ASSEMBLY

(a) Disconnect the shift control actuator assembly connector.

(b) Remove the 3 shift control actuator bolts and shift control actuator assembly from the hybrid vehicle transaxle assembly.

HINT:

As the position of the shift control actuator assembly is detected automatically, initialization is not necessary after disconnecting and reconnecting the cable to the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal.
