Title: LIGHTING (EXT): AFS (ADAPTIVE FRONT-LIGHTING SYSTEM): FREEZE FRAME DATA; 2019 - 2022 MY Prius [12/2018 -        ]

FREEZE FRAME DATA

FREEZE FRAME DATA

(a) Whenever an AFS (adaptive front-lighting system) DTC is stored, the No. 1 headlight ECU sub-assembly LH stores the current vehicle state as freeze frame data.

CHECK FREEZE FRAME DATA

(a) Connect the Techstream to the DLC3.

(b) Turn the power switch on (IG).

(c) Turn the Techstream on.

(d) Enter the following menus: Body Electrical / AFS / Trouble Codes.

Body Electrical > AFS > Trouble Codes

(e) Select a DTC to display its freeze frame data.

Body Electrical > AFS

CLEAR FREEZE FRAME DATA

NOTICE:

Clearing the DTCs will also clear the freeze frame data.

(a) Connect the Techstream to the DLC3.

(b) Turn the power switch on (IG).

(c) Clear the DTCs and freeze frame data.

(d) Enter the following menus: Body Electrical / AFS / Trouble Codes.

Body Electrical > AFS > Clear DTCs
