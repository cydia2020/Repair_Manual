5. REMOVE REAR FLEXIBLE HOSE

(a) Remove the union bolt and gasket.

(b) Remove the bolt and separate the rear flexible hose.

(c) Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the brake line while holding the rear flexible hose with a wrench.

NOTICE:

  • Do not kink or damage the brake line.
  • Do not allow any foreign matter such as dirt or dust to enter the brake line from the connecting parts.

(d) Remove the clip and rear flexible hose.
