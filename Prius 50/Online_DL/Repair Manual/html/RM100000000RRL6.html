Title: BRAKE CONTROL / DYNAMIC CONTROL SYSTEMS: ELECTRONICALLY CONTROLLED BRAKE SYSTEM: C1425; Open in Stop Switch Circuit; 2016 - 2018 MY Prius [11/2015 - 12/2018]

DTC

C1425

Open in Stop Switch Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The skid control ECU (brake booster with master cylinder assembly) detects the brake operating conditions through a signal transmitted by the stop light switch assembly.

The skid control ECU incorporates a circuit to detect an open circuit. This DTC is output when an open circuit is detected in the stop light signal input line.

Detection Item

INF Code

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

Note

C1425

Open in Stop Switch Circuit

231

An open stop light switch circuit continues for 10 seconds or more.

  • Stop light switch assembly
  • Skid control ECU (brake booster with master cylinder assembly)
  • Stop light circuit
