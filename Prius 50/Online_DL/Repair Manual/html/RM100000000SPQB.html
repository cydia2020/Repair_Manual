INSTALLATION

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL AIR FUEL RATIO SENSOR

HINT:

Perform "Inspection After Repair" after replacing the air fuel ratio sensor.

Click here

(a) Using SST, install the air fuel ratio sensor to the exhaust manifold (TWC: Front Catalyst).

SST: 09224-00011

Torque:

Specified tightening torque :

44 N·m {449 kgf·cm, 32 ft·lbf}

NOTICE:

If the air fuel ratio sensor has been struck or dropped, replace it.

HINT:

  • Calculate the torque wrench reading when changing the fulcrum length of the torque wrench.

    Click here

  • When using SST (fulcrum length of 30 mm (1.18 in.)) + torque wrench (fulcrum length of 255 mm (10.0 in.)):

    39 N*m (398 kgf*cm, 29 ft.*lbf)

*a

Torque Wrench Fulcrum Length

(b) Engage the 2 wire harness clamps.

(c) Connect the air fuel ratio sensor connector.
