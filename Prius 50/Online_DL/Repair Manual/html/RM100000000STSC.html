ECU Power Source Circuit

DESCRIPTION

This circuit supplies power to the driving support ECU assembly when the power switch is on (IG).

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following inspection procedure.
