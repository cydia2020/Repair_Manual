Title: AUDIO / VIDEO: AUDIO AND VISUAL SYSTEM: B1324,B1325; Lost Communication with Meter; 2020 - 2022 MY Prius [08/2019 -        ]

DTC

B1324

Lost Communication with Meter

DTC

B1325

Lost Communication with HUD

DESCRIPTION

These DTCs are stored when communication between the radio and display receiver assembly and meter circuit plate or combination meter mirror ECU* is not possible.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

B1324

Lost Communication with Meter

CAN reception error

  • Local bus communication line
  • Meter circuit plate power source circuit
  • Meter circuit plate
  • Radio and display receiver assembly
  • Harness or connector
  • No. 21 junction connector

B1325

Lost Communication with HUD

CAN reception error

  • Local bus communication line
  • Combination meter mirror ECU power source circuit
  • Combination meter mirror ECU
  • Radio and display receiver assembly
  • Harness or connector
  • No. 21 junction connector
  • *: w/ Headup Display System

HINT:

The radio and display receiver assembly is the master unit.
