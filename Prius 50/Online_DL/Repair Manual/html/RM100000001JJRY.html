Title: SUPPLEMENTAL RESTRAINT SYSTEMS: AIRBAG SYSTEM: B1633; Curtain Shield Airbag Sensor Initialization Error (RH); 2020 - 2022 MY Prius [08/2019 -        ]

DTC

B1633

Curtain Shield Airbag Sensor Initialization Error (RH)

DESCRIPTION

The side collision sensor RH circuit (bus 1) consists of the airbag ECU assembly, door side airbag sensor RH and rear airbag sensor RH.

The door side airbag sensor RH and rear airbag sensor RH detect impacts to the vehicle and send signals to the airbag ECU assembly to determine if the airbags and pretensioners should be deployed.

DTC B1633 is stored when a malfunction is detected in the side collision sensor RH circuit (bus 1).
