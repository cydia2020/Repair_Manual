Title: 2ZR-FXE (FUEL): FUEL PUMP: DISASSEMBLY; 2019 - 2022 MY Prius [12/2018 -        ]

DISASSEMBLY

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

Do not disconnect the tube shown in the illustration when disassembling the fuel suction tube with pump and gauge assembly. Doing so will cause reassembly of the fuel suction tube with pump and gauge assembly to be impossible as the tube is pressed into the fuel suction plate sub-assembly.

*a

Tube

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE FUEL SENDER GAUGE ASSEMBLY

Click here

2. REMOVE FUEL PUMP

(a) Disconnect the 2 fuel pump harness connectors.

(b) Disengage the clamp and remove the fuel pump harness from the fuel suction plate sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Do not damage the wire harness.
  • When disengaging each wire harness from the clamp, disengage one wire at a time.

(c) Disengage the claw and slide the fuel suction plate sub-assembly and then separate it from the No. 1 fuel sub-tank.

*1

Fuel Suction Plate Sub-assembly

Slide

(d) Disengage the 4 claws and remove the fuel suction plate sub-assembly from the No. 1 fuel sub-tank.

(e) Disengage the 3 claws and remove the No. 2 fuel suction support from the fuel tube supporter.

*a

Fuel Tube Supporter

(f) Remove the fuel pump from the fuel tube supporter.

(g) Remove the O-ring and fuel pump spacer from the fuel pump.
