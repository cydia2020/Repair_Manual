Navigation Voice Circuit

DESCRIPTION

This circuit is used when the voice switch of the steering pad switch assembly is pushed.

Using this circuit, the navigation receiver assembly sends signals to the stereo component amplifier assembly.

WIRING DIAGRAM

PROCEDURE

1.

CHECK HARNESS AND CONNECTOR (NAVIGATION RECEIVER ASSEMBLY - STEREO COMPONENT AMPLIFIER ASSEMBLY)

(a) Disconnect the F94 navigation receiver assembly connector.

(b) Disconnect the F117 stereo component amplifier assembly connector.
