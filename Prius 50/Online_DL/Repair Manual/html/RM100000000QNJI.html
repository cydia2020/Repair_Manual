Title: 2ZR-FXE (LUBRICATION): OIL PUMP: INSPECTION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT OIL PUMP RELIEF VALVE

(a) Coat the oil pump relief valve with engine oil, then check that it falls smoothly into the valve hole by its own weight.

HINT:

If the oil pump relief valve does not fall smoothly, replace the oil pump assembly.

2. INSPECT OIL PUMP ROTOR

(a) Check the side clearance.

(1) Using a feeler gauge and precision straightedge, measure the clearance between the oil pump drive rotor, oil pump driven rotor and precision straightedge.

(1) Using a feeler gauge, measure the clearance between the oil pump drive rotor and oil pump driven rotor tips.
