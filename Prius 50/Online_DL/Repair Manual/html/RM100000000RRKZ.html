C1403

Malfunction in Rear Speed Sensor RH Circuit

521

522

527

  • INF Code: 521
    • At a vehicle speed of 10 km/h (6 mph) or more, output voltage from one of the speed sensors is less than that from the other sensors for 15 seconds or more.
  • INF Code: 522
    • At a vehicle speed of 10 km/h (6 mph) or more, outputs from both speed sensors are 0 km/h (0 mph) for 15 seconds or more. (except when both rear speed sensors are 0 km/h (0 mph)).
  • INF Code: 527
    • At a vehicle speed of 10 km/h (6 mph) or more, output from one of the speed sensors is 0 km/h (0 mph) for 1 second or more.
  • Rear speed sensor RH (rear axle hub and bearing assembly RH)
  • Speed sensor installed improperly
  • Rear speed sensor rotor RH (rear axle hub and bearing assembly RH)
