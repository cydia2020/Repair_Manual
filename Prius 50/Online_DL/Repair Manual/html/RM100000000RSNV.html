Title: LIGHTING (EXT): REAR LIGHT ASSEMBLY: DISASSEMBLY; 2016 - 2018 MY Prius [11/2015 - 12/2018]

DISASSEMBLY

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

  • Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.
  • The following procedure is for the LH side.

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE REAR COMBINATION LIGHT LED

(a) Using a T20 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the screw.

Remove in this Direction

(b) Turn the rear combination light LED as shown in the illustration to remove it.

2. REMOVE REAR LIGHT LENS AND BODY
