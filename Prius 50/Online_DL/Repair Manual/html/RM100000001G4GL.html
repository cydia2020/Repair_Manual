Title: LIGHTING (EXT): LIGHTING SYSTEM (w/ AFS): Back-up Light Circuit; 2019 - 2022 MY Prius [12/2018 -        ]

Back-up Light Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The hybrid vehicle control ECU controls the back-up lights via the BKUP LP relay.

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

  • Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following procedure.
  • Before replacing the hybrid vehicle control ECU, refer to Registration.

(b) Turn the power switch on (IG).

(c) Turn the Techstream on.

(d) Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Hybrid Control / Trouble Codes.

(e) Check for DTCs.
