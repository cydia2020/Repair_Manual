Washer Fluid Level Warning Switch Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The combination meter assembly (meter circuit plate) and level warning switch assembly are connected via direct line. The combination meter assembly (meter circuit plate) determines the washer fluid level and outputs warning messages according to the washer fluid level warning switch status signal.
