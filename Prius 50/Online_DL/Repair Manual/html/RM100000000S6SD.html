Title: WINDOW / GLASS: POWER WINDOW REGULATOR MOTOR (for Front Door): INSPECTION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT POWER WINDOW REGULATOR MOTOR ASSEMBLY LH

(a) Connect a positive (+) auxiliary battery lead to connector terminal 2.

NOTICE:

Do not connect a positive (+) auxiliary battery lead to any terminals other than terminal 2 to avoid damaging the pulse sensor inside the motor.

*a

Component without harness connected

(Power Window Regulator Motor Assembly LH)

*b

Clockwise

*c

Counterclockwise

*d

Motor gear

(b) Connect a negative (-) auxiliary battery lead to connector terminals 1 and 7 or 10.

(c) Check that the motor gear rotates smoothly as follows:

OK:

Measurement Condition

Specified Condition
  1. Connect a positive (+) auxiliary battery lead to J2-2 (B), connect a negative (-) auxiliary battery lead to J2-1 (GND), and keep them connected for 3 seconds or more.
  2. With J2-2 (B) and J2-1 (GND) connected, connect a negative (-) auxiliary battery lead to J2-10 (UP).

Motor gear rotates clockwise
  1. Connect a positive (+) auxiliary battery lead to J2-2 (B), connect a negative (-) auxiliary battery lead to J2-1 (GND), and keep them connected for 3 seconds or more.
  2. With J2-2 (B) and J2-1 (GND) connected, connect a negative (-) auxiliary battery lead to J2-7 (DOWN).

Motor gear rotates counterclockwise
  • If the result is not as specified, replace the power window regulator motor assembly LH.

CAUTION:

Initialize the power window control system after installing the power window regulator motor and regulator assembly.
