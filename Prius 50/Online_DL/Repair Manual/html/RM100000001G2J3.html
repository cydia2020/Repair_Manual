Title: LIGHTING (EXT): REAR LIGHT ASSEMBLY: INSPECTION; 2019 - 2022 MY Prius [12/2018 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT REAR LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH

*a

Component without harness connected

(Rear Light Assembly LH)

(a) Illumination Inspection

(1) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the rear light assembly LH and check that the lights illuminate.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(W3)
Click Connector(W3)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

W3-1 (B) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

W3-2 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Rear light illuminates

If the result is not as specified, replace the rear light assembly LH.

2. INSPECT REAR LIGHT ASSEMBLY RH

*a

Component without harness connected

(Rear Light Assembly RH)

(a) Illumination Inspection

(1) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the rear light assembly RH and check that the lights illuminate.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(W2)
Click Connector(W2)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

W2-1 (B) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

W2-2 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Rear light illuminates

If the result is not as specified, replace the rear light assembly RH.
