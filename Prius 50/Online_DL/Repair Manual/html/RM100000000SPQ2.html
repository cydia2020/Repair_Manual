8. REMOVE IGNITION COIL ASSEMBLY

(a) Disconnect the 4 ignition coil assembly connectors.

(b) Remove the 4 bolts and 4 ignition coil assemblies from the cylinder head cover sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

If an ignition coil assembly has been struck or dropped, replace it.

HINT:

Arrange the removed parts in the correct order.

9. REMOVE SPARK PLUG

(a) Remove the 4 spark plugs from the cylinder head sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

If a spark plug has been struck or dropped, replace it.

HINT:

Arrange the removed parts in the correct order.
