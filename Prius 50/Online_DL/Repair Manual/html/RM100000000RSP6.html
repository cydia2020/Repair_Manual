Title: LIGHTING (INT): VANITY LIGHT: REMOVAL; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

REMOVAL

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

  • Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.
  • The following procedure is for the LH side.

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE VANITY LIGHT ASSEMBLY

(a) Using a moulding remover, disengage the claw.

(b) Disengage the 2 claws to remove the vanity light assembly from the bulb holder.

2. REMOVE VANITY LIGHT BULB

(a) Remove the vanity light bulb from the bulb holder as shown in the illustration.
