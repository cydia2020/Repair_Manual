2. REMOVE REAR SIDE ULTRASONIC SENSOR

HINT:

The illustration is for the LH side. The orientation for the RH side is the opposite of the LH side.

(a) Disconnect the connector.

(b) Disengage the 2 claws and remove the rear side ultrasonic sensor as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction

3. REMOVE REAR CORNER ULTRASONIC SENSOR

HINT:

The illustration is for the LH side. The orientation for the RH side is the opposite of the LH side.

(a) Disconnect the connector.

(b) Disengage the 2 claws and remove the rear corner ultrasonic sensor as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction

4. REMOVE REAR CENTER ULTRASONIC SENSOR

HINT:

The illustration is for the LH side. The orientation for the RH side is the opposite of the LH side.

(a) Disconnect the connector.

(b) Disengage the 2 claws and remove the rear center ultrasonic sensor as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction

5. REMOVE REAR SIDE ULTRASONIC SENSOR RETAINER

HINT:

  • Perform this procedure only when replacement of the rear side ultrasonic sensor retainer is necessary.
  • When removing the rear side ultrasonic sensor retainer, heat the rear bumper assembly and rear side ultrasonic sensor retainer using a heat light.

(a) Heat the rear bumper assembly and rear side ultrasonic sensor retainer using a heat light at the specified temperature for 3 to 5 minutes.
