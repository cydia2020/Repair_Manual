Title: PARK ASSIST / MONITORING: BLIND SPOT MONITOR SYSTEM: Power Source Circuit; 2016 - 2018 MY Prius [11/2015 - 12/2018]

Power Source Circuit

DESCRIPTION

This circuit provides power to operate the blind spot monitor sensor.
