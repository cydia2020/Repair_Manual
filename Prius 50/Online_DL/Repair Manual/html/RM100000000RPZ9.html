Title: 2ZR-FXE (FUEL): FUEL PUMP: DISASSEMBLY; 2016 - 2018 MY Prius [11/2015 - 12/2018]

DISASSEMBLY

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

  • Do not disconnect the tube shown in the illustration when disassembling the fuel suction tube with pump and gauge assembly. Doing so will cause reassembly of the fuel suction tube with pump and gauge assembly to be impossible as the tube is pressed into the fuel suction plate sub-assembly.
  • When replacing the fuel filter, replace it together with the fuel suction plate sub-assembly.

2. REMOVE FUEL PUMP WITH FILTER ASSEMBLY

(a) Disengage the clamp to disconnect the fuel pump harness from the fuel suction plate sub-assembly.

(b) Disconnect the fuel pump harness connector.

(c) Using needle nose pliers, remove the E-ring from the fuel suction plate shaft.

(d) Disengage the claw and clamp and disconnect the fuel pump filter hose from the No. 1 fuel sub-tank.

(e) Disengage the 2 claws and remove the fuel filter and fuel pump with filter assembly from the No. 1 fuel sub-tank.

NOTICE:

Do not do anything which may separate the tube from either the fuel suction plate sub-assembly or fuel filter, such as applying excessive force to the tube.

Click here

(f) Remove the 2 springs from the fuel suction plate shafts.

(g) Using a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape, disengage the 2 claws and remove the No. 1 fuel suction support from the fuel filter.

*a

Protective Tape

(h) Using a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape, disengage the 5 claws, and remove the fuel pump with filter assembly from the fuel filter.

NOTICE:

  • Do not damage the fuel filter or suction filter.
  • Do not remove the suction filter from the fuel pump with filter assembly.
  • Do not do anything which may separate the tube from either the fuel suction plate sub-assembly or fuel filter, such as applying excessive force to the tube.

(i) Disconnect the fuel pump harness connector to remove the fuel pump harness from the fuel pump with filter assembly.

(j) Remove the O-ring and fuel pump spacer from the fuel pump with filter assembly.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the sealing surface.

HINT:

If the O-ring still remains in the fuel filter, remove it using a wire tip (1 mm (0.0394 in.) diameter) that is formed as shown in the illustration.
