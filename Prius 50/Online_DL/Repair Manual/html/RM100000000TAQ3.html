FREEZE FRAME DATA

CHECK FREEZE FRAME DATA

HINT:

The hybrid vehicle control ECU records vehicle and driving condition information as freeze frame data the moment a DTC is stored. Freeze frame data can help determine the vehicle conditions when the malfunction occurred and help duplicate conditions when troubleshooting.

(a) Connect the Techstream to the DLC3.

(b) Turn the power switch on (IG).

(c) Turn the Techstream on.

(d) Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Hybrid Control / Trouble Codes.

(e) Select a DTC in order to display its freeze frame data.

Powertrain > Hybrid Control > Trouble Codes
