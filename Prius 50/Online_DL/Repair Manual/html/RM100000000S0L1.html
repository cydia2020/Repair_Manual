REMOVAL

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

  • Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.
  • The following procedure is for the LH side.

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE QUARTER PILLAR COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY

HINT:

When removing the quarter pillar cover sub-assembly, heat the vehicle body and quarter pillar cover sub-assembly using a heat light.
