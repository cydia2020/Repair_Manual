Clearance Warning Buzzer Circuit

DESCRIPTION

This circuit consists of the No. 1 clearance warning buzzer, No. 2 clearance warning buzzer and clearance warning ECU assembly. An ECU-excited type buzzer is used. The ECU operates the buzzers using a sound pattern that changes depending on the distance to the obstacle.
