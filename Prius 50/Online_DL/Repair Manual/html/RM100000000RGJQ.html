Title: POWER OUTLETS (INT): WIRELESS CHARGING SYSTEM: Wireless Charger Illumination Circuit; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

Wireless Charger Illumination Circuit

DESCRIPTION

When the light control switch is turned to the tail or head position, this circuit sends an illumination signal to the mobile wireless charger cradle assembly.

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following procedure.
