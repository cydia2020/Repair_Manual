Title: CRUISE CONTROL: CRUISE CONTROL SYSTEM: P057162; Brake Switch "A" Signal Compare Failure; 2016 - 2018 MY Prius [11/2015 - 12/2018]

DTC

P057162

Brake Switch "A" Signal Compare Failure

DESCRIPTION

When the brake pedal is depressed, the stop light switch assembly sends a signal to the hybrid vehicle control ECU. When the hybrid vehicle control ECU receives this signal, it cancels control of vehicle speed by the cruise control system. The fail-safe function operates to enable normal driving even if there is a malfunction in the stop light switch assembly signal circuit. The cancellation condition occurs when voltage is applied to terminal STP. When the brake pedal is depressed, voltage is applied to terminal STP of the hybrid vehicle control ECU through the STOP fuse and the stop light switch assembly, and the hybrid vehicle control ECU cancels control of vehicle speed by the cruise control system.

HINT:

After performing repairs on the vehicle, clear the DTCs and perform the following procedure to check that DTCs are not output.

  1. Turn the power switch on (IG).
  2. Turn the cruise control system on using the cruise control main switch (ON-OFF button).
  3. Depress the brake pedal for 0.5 seconds or more.
