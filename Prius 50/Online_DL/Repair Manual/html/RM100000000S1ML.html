7. REMOVE REAR WIPER ARM AND BLADE ASSEMBLY

(a) Slide the rear wiper arm head cap as shown in the illustration to disengage the 2 claws.

(b) Disengage the 2 claws and open the rear wiper arm head cap as shown in the illustration.

(c) Remove the nut and rear wiper arm and blade assembly.

8. REMOVE REAR WIPER MOTOR GROMMET

(a) Remove the rear wiper motor grommet.

9. REMOVE REAR WIPER MOTOR ASSEMBLY

(a) for Type A:

(1) Disconnect the connector.

(2) Remove the 3 bolts and rear wiper motor assembly.

HINT:

Remove the bolts and rear wiper motor assembly as a set.

(b) for Type B:

(1) Disconnect the connector.

(2) Remove the 3 bolts and rear wiper motor assembly.

HINT:

Remove the bolts (A) and rear wiper motor assembly as a set.
