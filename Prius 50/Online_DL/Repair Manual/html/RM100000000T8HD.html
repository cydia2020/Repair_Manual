Cursor or Map Rotates when Vehicle Stopped

PROCEDURE

1.

CHECK CONDITION

(a) Check with the customer if the vehicle has been turned by a turntable.

OK:

Vehicle has not been turned by a turntable.

HINT:

If the vehicle is turned on a turntable with the power switch turned on (ACC) or (IG), the system may store the angular velocity. As a result, the vehicle position cursor may deviate. In this case, the vehicle position cursor will return to normal by turning the power switch off and back on (ACC) or (IG) again with the vehicle completely stopped.

OK

PROCEED TO NEXT SUSPECTED AREA SHOWN IN PROBLEM SYMPTOMS TABLE

NG

TURN POWER SWITCH ON (ACC) OR (IG) WHEN VEHICLE IS COMPLETELY STOPPED
