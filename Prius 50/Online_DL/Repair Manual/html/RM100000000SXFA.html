Title: PARK ASSIST / MONITORING: INTUITIVE PARKING ASSIST SYSTEM: C1AF8; Rr IPA Sensor Communication Circuit; 2016 - 2018 MY Prius [11/2015 - 12/2018]

DTC

C1AF8

Rr IPA Sensor Communication Circuit

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when there is an open or short circuit in the communication line between the rear side sensors and the ECU, or when there is a malfunction in a rear side sensor.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

C1AF8

Rr IPA Sensor Communication Circuit

An open or short circuit in the communication line between the rear side sensors and ECU or a malfunction in a rear side sensor during initialization mode after the power switch is turned on (IG).

  • Rear side ultrasonic sensor circuit
  • Harness or connector
  • Clearance warning ECU assembly
