Title: CELLULAR COMMUNICATION: SAFETY CONNECT SYSTEM (for 7 Inch Display): B153711,B153713; Telephone Sub Antenna Circuit Short to Ground; 2021 - 2022 MY Prius [07/2020 -        ]

DTC

B153711

Telephone Sub Antenna Circuit Short to Ground

DTC

B153713

Telephone Sub Antenna Circuit Open

DESCRIPTION

These DTCs are stored when a malfunction occurs in the telephone and GPS antenna (for Front Side).

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

B153711

Telephone Sub Antenna Circuit Short to Ground

Telephone antenna (sub) impedance (Ω) is lower than the malfunction threshold for 10 seconds or more when the power switch is on (IG)

(Short circuit)

  • DCM (telematics transceiver)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna (for Front Side)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (Antenna cord sub-assembly (for Type A))
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (Antenna cord sub-assembly (for Type B))
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (Antenna cord sub-assembly (for Type C))

B153713

Telephone Sub Antenna Circuit Open

Telephone antenna (sub) impedance (Ω) is higher than the malfunction threshold for 10 seconds or more when the power switch is on (IG)

(Open circuit)

  • DCM (telematics transceiver)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna (for Front Side)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (Antenna cord sub-assembly (for Type A))
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (Antenna cord sub-assembly (for Type B))
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (Antenna cord sub-assembly (for Type C))

PROCEDURE

1.

CHECK DTC

(a) Turn the power switch off.

(b) Connect the Techstream to the DLC3.

(c) Turn the power switch on (IG) and wait for 10 seconds or more.

(d) Turn the Techstream on.

(e) Clear the DTCs.

Body Electrical > Telematics > Clear DTCs

(f) Check for DTCs and check that no DTCs are output.

Body Electrical > Telematics > Trouble Codes

OK:

No DTCs are output.
