Title: LIGHTING (EXT): REAR LIGHT ASSEMBLY: INSPECTION; 2016 - 2018 MY Prius [11/2015 - 12/2018]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT REAR LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH

(a) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the rear light assembly LH and check that the light comes on.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(P12)
Click Connector(P12)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

P12-2 (B) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

P12-1 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Taillight comes on

If the result is not as specified, replace the rear light lens and body LH.

*a

Component without harness connected

(Rear Light Assembly LH (Taillight))

(b) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the rear light assembly LH and check that the light comes on.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(P10)
Click Connector(P10)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

P10-2 (B) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

P10-1 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Stop light comes on

If the result is not as specified, replace the rear combination light LED.
