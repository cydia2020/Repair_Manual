(c) Remove the 2 bolts and sliding roof drive gear sub-assembly.

2. REMOVE SUNSHADE TRIM SUB-ASSEMBLY

(a) Remove the screw.

Remove in this Direction (1)

Remove in this Direction (2)

(c) Move the sliding roof piece sub-assembly LH in the direction indicated by the arrow (2) shown in the illustration to disengage the 2 guides (B) and remove the sliding roof piece sub-assembly LH.
