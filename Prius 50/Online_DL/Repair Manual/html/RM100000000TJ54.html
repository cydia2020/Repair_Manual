8. REMOVE ENGINE COOLANT TEMPERATURE SENSOR

(a) Disconnect the engine coolant temperature sensor connector.

(b) Using a 19 mm union nut wrench, remove the engine coolant temperature sensor from the No. 2 water joint.

NOTICE:

Do not apply any excessive force to the heater pipe when removing the engine coolant temperature sensor.
