Title: LIGHTING (EXT): HEADLIGHT ASSEMBLY: DISASSEMBLY; 2016 - 2018 MY Prius [11/2015 - 12/2018]

DISASSEMBLY

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

  • Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.
  • The following procedure is for the LH side.

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE NO. 1 HEADLIGHT COVER

(a) Turn the No. 1 headlight cover as shown in the illustration to remove it.

Remove in this Direction

2. REMOVE FRONT TURN SIGNAL LIGHT BULB

(a) Turn the front turn signal light socket with the front turn signal light bulb as shown in the illustration to remove them as a unit.

Remove in this Direction

(b) Remove the front turn signal light bulb from the front turn signal light socket.

3. REMOVE FRONT TURN SIGNAL LIGHT SOCKET

4. REMOVE HEADLIGHT UNIT ASSEMBLY
