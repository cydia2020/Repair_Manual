Title: LIGHTING (INT): FRONT DOOR COURTESY SWITCH: REMOVAL; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

REMOVAL

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

  • Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.
  • The following procedure is for the LH side.

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE FRONT DOOR COURTESY LIGHT SWITCH ASSEMBLY

(a) Using a T30 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the screw.

(b) Disconnect the connector to remove the front door courtesy light switch assembly.
