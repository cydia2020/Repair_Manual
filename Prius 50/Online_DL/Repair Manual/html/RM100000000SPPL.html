Title: 2ZR-FXE (ENGINE CONTROL): ECM: INSTALLATION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSTALLATION

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL NO. 2 ECM BRACKET

(a) Install the No. 2 ECM bracket to the ECM with the 2 screws.

Torque:

4.5 N·m {46 kgf·cm, 40 in·lbf}

2. INSTALL NO. 1 ECM BRACKET

(a) Install the No. 1 ECM bracket to the ECM with the 2 screws.

Torque:

4.5 N·m {46 kgf·cm, 40 in·lbf}

3. INSTALL ECM

(a) Install the ECM with the bolt and nut.

Torque:

Bolt :

13 N·m {133 kgf·cm, 10 ft·lbf}

Nut :

8.0 N·m {82 kgf·cm, 71 in·lbf}

NOTICE:

If the ECM has been struck or dropped, replace it.

(b) Install the engine wire to the No. 1 ECM bracket with the bolt.

Torque:

8.0 N·m {82 kgf·cm, 71 in·lbf}

(c) Engage the 2 clamps.

(d) Connect the 2 ECM connectors and lower the 2 levers.

NOTICE:

  • When connecting the ECM connectors, make sure that the connecting parts of the ECM connectors are free of dirt, water or other foreign matter.
  • Be sure to securely connect the ECM connectors.
