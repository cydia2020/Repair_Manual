DTC

B1350

Speaker Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The vehicle approaching speaker assembly circuit consists of the hybrid vehicle control ECU and vehicle approaching speaker assembly.

This DTC is stored when a malfunction is detected in the vehicle approaching speaker assembly circuit.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

Note

B1350

Speaker Circuit

Either of the following conditions is met:

  • When sound output voltage is being monitored by the ECU, with the power switch on (IG), battery voltage 10 V or more, and warning sound being produced, a speaker circuit short condition continues for 1 second or more.
  • When sound output voltage is being monitored by the ECU, with the power switch on (IG) and warning sound not being produced, a speaker circuit open condition continues for 5 seconds or more.
  • Hybrid vehicle control ECU
  • Vehicle approaching speaker assembly
  • Harness or connector between hybrid vehicle control ECU and vehicle approaching speaker assembly

If the vehicle approaching speaker controller stores a DTC, the multi-information display "Proximity Notification System Malfunction Visit Your Dealer" on displayed.
