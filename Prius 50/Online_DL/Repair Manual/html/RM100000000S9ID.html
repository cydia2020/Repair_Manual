PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE FRONT DRIVE SHAFT ASSEMBLY

Click here

2. REMOVE FRONT DRIVE SHAFT OIL SEAL LH

(a) Using SST, remove the front drive shaft oil seal LH from the hybrid vehicle transaxle assembly.

SST: 09308-00010

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the hybrid vehicle transaxle assembly.

3. REMOVE FRONT DRIVE SHAFT OIL SEAL RH

(a) Using SST, remove the front drive shaft oil seal RH from the hybrid vehicle transaxle assembly.

SST: 09308-00010

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the hybrid vehicle transaxle assembly.

4. INSTALL FRONT DRIVE SHAFT OIL SEAL RH

(a) Using SST and a hammer, install a new front drive shaft oil seal RH to the hybrid vehicle transaxle assembly.

SST: 09710-30050

SST: 09950-70010

09951-07100

Standard Depth:

-0.5 to 0.5 mm (-0.0197 to 0.0197 in.)

NOTICE:

  • Do not allow foreign matter to attach to the lip of the front drive shaft oil seal RH.
  • Do not install the front drive shaft oil seal RH at an angle.

*a

Depth

(b) Coat the lip of the front drive shaft oil seal RH with a small amount of MP grease.

5. INSTALL FRONT DRIVE SHAFT OIL SEAL LH

(a) Using SST and a hammer, install a new front drive shaft oil seal LH to the hybrid vehicle transaxle assembly.

SST: 09649-17010

SST: 09950-70010

09951-07100

Standard Depth:

-0.5 to 0.5 mm (-0.0197 to 0.0197 in.)

NOTICE:

  • Do not allow foreign matter to attach to the lip of the front drive shaft oil seal LH.
  • Do not install the front drive shaft oil seal LH at an angle.

*a

Depth

(b) Coat the lip of the front drive shaft oil seal LH with a small amount of MP grease.
