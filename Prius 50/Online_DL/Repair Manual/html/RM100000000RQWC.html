Title: PARK ASSIST / MONITORING: BLIND SPOT MONITOR SYSTEM: C1ABD,C1ABE; Short to +B in Buzzer; 2016 - 2018 MY Prius [11/2015 - 12/2018]

DTC

C1ABD

Short to +B in Buzzer

DTC

C1ABE

Short to GND or Open in Buzzer

DESCRIPTION

  • DTC C1ABD is stored when the blind spot monitor sensor RH detects a +B short in the RCTA buzzer (blind spot monitor buzzer) circuit.
  • DTC C1ABE is stored when the blind spot monitor sensor RH detects a ground short or open in the RCTA buzzer (blind spot monitor buzzer) circuit.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

C1ABD

Short to +B in Buzzer

Both of the following conditions are met:

  • The blind spot monitor system is on.
  • The blind spot monitor sensor is not sending voltage to the buzzer but the buzzer receives voltage above a specified level for a specified period of time.
  • Blind spot monitor sensor RH
  • RCTA buzzer (blind spot monitor buzzer)
  • Harness or connector

C1ABE

Short to GND or Open in Buzzer

Both of the following conditions are met:

  • The blind spot monitor system is on.
  • The current flowing to the buzzer is below a specified value when buzzer operation voltage is being sent to the buzzer.
  • Blind spot monitor sensor RH
  • RCTA buzzer (blind spot monitor buzzer)
  • Harness or connector

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

  • When checking for DTCs, make sure that the blind spot monitor system is turned on.
  • Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following inspection procedure.
