Title: 2ZR-FXE (ENGINE CONTROL): RELAY: ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT CIRCUIT OPENING RELAY ASSEMBLY (C/OPN)

(a) Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

Standard Resistance:

Tester Connection

Condition

Specified Condition

3 - 5

Auxiliary battery voltage is not applied between terminals 1 and 2

10 kΩ or higher

3 - 5

Auxiliary battery voltage is applied between terminals 1 and 2

Below 1 Ω

If the result is not as specified, replace the circuit opening relay assembly (C/OPN).

2. INSPECT WATER PUMP RELAY (ENG W/PMP)

(a) Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

Standard Resistance:

Tester Connection

Condition

Specified Condition

3 - 5

Auxiliary battery voltage is not applied between terminals 1 and 2

10 kΩ or higher

3 - 5

Auxiliary battery voltage is applied between terminals 1 and 2

Below 1 Ω

If the result is not as specified, replace the water pump relay (ENG W/PMP).
