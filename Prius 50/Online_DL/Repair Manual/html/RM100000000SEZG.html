2. REMOVE FUEL SENDER GAUGE ASSEMBLY

(a) Disconnect the fuel sender gauge assembly connector.

(b) Disengage the claw to remove the fuel sender gauge assembly from the fuel suction tube with pump and gauge assembly.

NOTICE:
