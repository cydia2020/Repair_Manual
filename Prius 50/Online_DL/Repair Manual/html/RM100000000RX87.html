INSTALLATION

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL INVERTER BATTERY

(a) Temporarily install the inverter battery with the 2 bolts.

(b) Fully tighten the 2 bolts in the order shown in the illustration.

Torque:

12.5 N·m {127 kgf·cm, 9 ft·lbf}

(c) Engage the 2 clamps.

(d) Connect the inverter battery connector.
