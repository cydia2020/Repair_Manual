Title: 2ZR-FXE (FUEL): FUEL PUMP: REASSEMBLY; 2016 - 2018 MY Prius [11/2015 - 12/2018]

REASSEMBLY

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL FUEL PUMP WITH FILTER ASSEMBLY

HINT:

Perform "Inspection After Repair" after replacing the fuel pump with filter assembly.

Click here

(a) Apply gasoline to a new O-ring. Then install the O-ring and fuel pump spacer to the fuel pump with filter assembly.

*1

O-ring

*2

Fuel Pump Spacer

(b) Connect the fuel pump harness connector to install the fuel pump harness to the fuel pump with filter assembly.

(c) Engage the 5 claws to install the fuel pump with filter assembly to the fuel filter.

NOTICE:

  • Make sure that the O-ring is not cut or pinched during the installation.
  • Engage the claws securely.
  • Do not remove the suction filter.

(d) Engage the 2 claws to install the No. 1 fuel suction support to the fuel filter.

(e) Install the 2 springs to the fuel suction plate shafts.

(f) Engage the 2 claws and install the fuel filter and fuel pump with filter assembly to the No. 1 fuel sub-tank.

NOTICE:

Do not apply excessive force to the tube or No. 1 fuel suction support.

(g) Engage the clamp and claw, and connect the fuel pump filter hose while aligning it with the installation position of the fuel sub-tank.

(h) Install a new E-ring to the fuel suction plate shaft.

(i) Connect the fuel pump harness connector.

(j) Engage the clamp to connect the fuel pump harness to the fuel suction plate sub-assembly.
