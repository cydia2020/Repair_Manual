Title: WIPER / WASHER: WASHER MOTOR: INSPECTION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT WINDSHIELD WASHER MOTOR AND PUMP ASSEMBLY

(a) for Front Washer:

(1) Remove the washer jar.

(2) Disconnect the washer motor and pump assembly connector.

HINT:

This check should be performed with the washer motor and pump assembly installed to the washer jar.

(3) Fill the washer jar with washer fluid.

(4) Check that washer fluid flows from the washer motor and pump assembly.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(A33)
Click Connector(A33)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

A33-1 - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

A33-2 - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Washer motor and pump assembly operates and washer fluid flows from the washer motor and pump assembly.

If the result is not as specified, replace the washer motor and pump assembly.

*a

Component without harness connected

(Washer Motor and Pump Assembly)

(b) for Rear Washer (w/ Rear Wiper System):

(1) Remove the washer jar.

(2) Disconnect the washer motor and pump assembly connector.

HINT:

This check should be performed with the washer motor and pump assembly installed to the washer jar.

(3) Fill the washer jar with washer fluid.

(4) Check that the washer motor and pump assembly operates and washer fluid flows from the washer motor and pump assembly.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(A33)
Click Connector(A33)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

A33-2 - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

A33-1 - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Washer motor and pump assembly operates and washer fluid flows from the washer motor and pump assembly.

If the result is not as specified, replace the washer motor and pump assembly.

*a

Component without harness connected

(Washer Motor and Pump Assembly)
