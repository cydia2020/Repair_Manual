Title: HEATING / AIR CONDITIONING: REFRIGERANT LINE: INSTALLATION; 2016 - 2020 MY Prius [11/2015 - 07/2020]

INSTALLATION

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL PIPING CLAMP (for Suction Hose Sub-assembly)

(a) Remove the vinyl tape from the open ends of the suction pipe sub-assembly and suction hose sub-assembly.

(b) Install a new piping clamp to the suction hose sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Securely engage the inside step of the piping clamp in the suction hose sub-assembly groove.
  • Do not widen the opening of the piping clamp more than the diameter of the suction hose sub-assembly when installing it.
  • Do not install the piping clamp with the large diameter section in the wrong direction.

Suction Hose Sub-assembly

*2

Piping Clamp

*a

Groove

(c) Thoroughly coat 2 new O-rings and the contact surface of the suction hose sub-assembly with compressor oil.

Compressor Oil:

ND-OIL 11 or equivalent

(d) Install the 2 O-rings to the suction hose sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

Do not let foreign matter adhere to the O-rings or O-ring seals.

(e) Engage the suction pipe sub-assembly and suction hose sub-assembly by matching the alignment marks.

NOTICE:

Engage the parts by holding the pipe, not the piping clamp.

Large Diameter Section of Piping Clamp

*b

Alignment Mark

(f) Securely insert the piping clamp to the point where the large diameter section of the piping clamp is covered by the suction pipe sub-assembly.

HINT:

  • When inserting, make sure that a click sound is heard.
  • Check that the suction hose sub-assembly is securely inserted by pulling it.
