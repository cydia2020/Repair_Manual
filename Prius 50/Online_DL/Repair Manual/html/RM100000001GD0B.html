Title: SEAT: SEAT HEATER SWITCH: INSPECTION; 2019 - 2022 MY Prius [12/2018 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT SEAT HEATER SWITCH (for LH)

(a) Check that the LEDs illuminate.

(1) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the seat heater switch (for LH) and check that each LED illuminates.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(F108)
Click Connector(F108)
