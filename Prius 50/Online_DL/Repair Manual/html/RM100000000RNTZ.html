REMOVAL

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE INNER REAR VIEW MIRROR ASSEMBLY (w/o Toyota Safety Sense)

(a) Disengage the 2 claws and disconnect the inner mirror base cover.

(b) Pull the lock lever in the directon indicated by the arrow (1) in the illustration, and slide the inner rear view mirror assembly in the direction indicated by the arrow (2) to remove it.
