Title: EXTERIOR PANELS / TRIM: ROOF DRIP SIDE FINISH MOULDING: REMOVAL; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

REMOVAL

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

  • Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.
  • The following procedure is for the LH side.

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE CENTER ROOF DRIP SIDE FINISH MOULDING

(a) Apply protective tape around the center roof drip side finish moulding.

Protective Tape

-

-

(b) Using a moulding remover, disengage the 2 clips and remove the center roof drip side finish moulding.

NOTICE:

  • Do not remove the roof drip side finish moulding clips.
  • If a roof drip side finish moulding clip is damaged or falls off, replace it with a new one.
