2. REMOVE PIPING CLAMP (for Suction Hose Sub-assembly)

(a) While pressing the end of the suction hose sub-assembly into the end of the suction pipe sub-assembly, use pliers to squeeze together both sides of the piping clamp until it breaks apart.

NOTICE:

  • If any foreign matter is adhered to the connecting part, brush it off or use compressed air to remove it.
  • Make sure that fragments of the piping clamp do not enter the piping.

(b) Disconnect the suction hose sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

Clean off any foreign matter near the ends of the suction hose sub-assembly and suction pipe sub-assembly.

(c) Remove the 2 O-rings from the suction hose sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

Wrap the open ends of the separated suction pipe sub-assembly and suction hose sub-assembly with vinyl tape to prevent entry of moisture and foreign matter.

(d) Remove the piping clamp.
