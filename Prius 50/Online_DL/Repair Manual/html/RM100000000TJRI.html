INSTALLATION

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL OCCUPANT DETECTION ECU

(a) Engage the claw to install the occupant detection ECU.

NOTICE:

If the occupant detection ECU has been dropped, or there are any cracks, dents or other defects in the case or connector, replace the occupant detection ECU with a new one.

(b) Connect the 2 connectors.

NOTICE:

When installing the occupant detection ECU, be careful that the SRS wiring does not interfere with or is not pinched between other parts.
