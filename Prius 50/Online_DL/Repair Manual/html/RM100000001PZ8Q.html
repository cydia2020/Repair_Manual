Title: HEATING / AIR CONDITIONING: AIR CONDITIONING SYSTEM: B1279; Lost Communication with Humidity/Rain Sensor LIN; 2021 - 2022 MY Prius [07/2020 -        ]

DTC

B1279

Lost Communication with Humidity/Rain Sensor LIN

DESCRIPTION

The air conditioning amplifier assembly and rain sensor communicate via LIN communication.

The air conditioning amplifier assembly stores this DTC if communication becomes abnormal.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

Memory

B1279

Lost Communication with Humidity/Rain Sensor LIN

Lost communication with rain sensor

  • Harness or connector between rain sensor and IG power source
  • Harness or connector between rain sensor and body ground
  • LIN communication system
  • Air conditioning amplifier assembly
  • Rain sensor

Memorized

(10 sec. or more)*

  • *: The air conditioning amplifier assembly stores this DTC if the malfunction has occurred for the period of time indicated in the brackets.
