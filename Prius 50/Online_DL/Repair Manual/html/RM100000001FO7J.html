3. REMOVE DIFFERENTIAL SIDE GEAR SHAFT SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

(a) Secure SST to the differential side gear shaft sub-assembly LH with 2 nuts as shown in the illustration and remove the differential side gear shaft sub-assembly LH from the rear traction motor with transaxle assembly.

SST: 09520-24010

09520-04010

09520-32040

NOTICE:

Do not damage the differential side gear shaft LH type T oil seal.

(b) Secure the differential side gear shaft sub-assembly LH in a vise between aluminum plates.

NOTICE:

Do not overtighten the vise.

(c) Using 2 screwdrivers and a hammer, remove the snap ring from the differential side gear shaft sub-assembly LH.

NOTICE:

Use a piece of cloth to keep the snap ring from flying off.

4. REMOVE DIFFERENTIAL SIDE GEAR SHAFT SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:

Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.

5. REMOVE DIFFERENTIAL SIDE GEAR SHAFT DUST COVER LH

(a) Secure the differential side gear shaft sub-assembly LH in a vise between aluminum plates.

NOTICE:

Do not overtighten the vise.

(b) Using a screwdriver and hammer, remove the differential side gear shaft dust cover LH from the differential side gear shaft sub-assembly LH.

NOTICE:

Do not damage the differential side gear shaft sub-assembly LH.

6. REMOVE DIFFERENTIAL SIDE GEAR SHAFT DUST COVER RH

HINT:

Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.
