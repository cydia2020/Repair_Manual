Title: DOOR LOCK: WIRELESS DOOR LOCK BUZZER: REMOVAL; 2019 - 2022 MY Prius [12/2018 -        ]

REMOVAL

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE FRONT WHEEL LH

Click here

2. REMOVE FRONT FENDER LINER LH

(a) Remove the 3 screws.

(b) Remove the 5 screws and grommet.

(c) Using a screwdriver, turn each pin 90 degrees and remove the 2 pin hold clips.

*a

90°

(d) for Rough Road Area Specification Vehicles:

(1) Remove the clip.

(e) Remove the 10 clips and front fender liner LH.

3. REMOVE WIRELESS DOOR LOCK BUZZER

(a) Disconnect the connector.

(b) Disengage the clamp to remove the wireless door lock buzzer.
