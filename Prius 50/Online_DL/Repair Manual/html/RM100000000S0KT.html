Title: EXTERIOR PANELS / TRIM: GRILLE SHUTTER: DISASSEMBLY; 2016 - 2018 MY Prius [11/2015 - 12/2018]

DISASSEMBLY

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE MOTOR COVER

(a) Disengage the 4 claws as shown in the illustration to remove the motor cover.

Remove in this Direction

2. REMOVE SWING GRILLE ACTUATOR ASSEMBLY

(a) Disconnect the connector and remove the swing grille actuator assembly as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction

3. REMOVE NO. 5 RADIATOR GRILLE SEAL

(a) Remove the No. 5 radiator grille seal.

4. REMOVE RADIATOR GRILLE SEAL

(a) Remove the radiator grille seal.

5. REMOVE NO. 3 RADIATOR GRILLE SEAL

(a) Remove the No. 3 radiator grille seal.

6. REMOVE NO. 4 RADIATOR GRILLE SEAL

(a) Remove the No. 4 radiator grille seal.

7. REMOVE NO. 2 RADIATOR GRILLE SEAL

(a) Remove the No. 2 radiator grille seal.

8. REMOVE NO. 6 RADIATOR GRILLE SEAL

(a) Remove the No. 6 radiator grille seal.
