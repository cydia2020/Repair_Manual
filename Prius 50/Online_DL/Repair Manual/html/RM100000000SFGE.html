Title: SEAT: REAR SEAT ASSEMBLY: INSTALLATION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSTALLATION

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:

Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL REAR SEAT CUSHION LOCK HOOK

(a) Engage the 2 claws to install a new rear seat cushion lock hook as shown in the illustration.

HINT:

Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.

Install in this Direction

2. INSTALL REAR SEAT CUSHION ASSEMBLY

(a) Place the rear seat cushion assembly in the cabin.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the vehicle body.

(b) Engage the rear seat cushion frame hook on the rear side of the rear seat cushion assembly as shown in the illustration.

*a

Rear Seat Cushion Frame Hook Rear Side

Install in this Direction

NOTICE:

Do not engage the rear seat cushion frame hooks on the front side of the rear seat cushion assembly. They will be engaged after connecting the rear center seat outer belt assembly.

3. INSTALL REAR SEATBACK ASSEMBLY LH

(a) Place the rear seatback assembly RH in the cabin.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the vehicle body.

(b) Engage the 2 guides.

(c) Install the rear seatback assembly RH with the 2 bolts.

Torque:

36.8 N·m {375 kgf·cm, 27 ft·lbf}

(d) Engage the 2 fasteners.

(e) Pull back the rear seatback assembly LH.

4. INSTALL REAR SEATBACK ASSEMBLY RH

(a) Place the rear seatback assembly RH in the cabin.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the vehicle body.

(b) Engage the 2 guides.

(c) Install the rear seatback assembly RH with the 2 bolts.

Torque:

36.8 N·m {375 kgf·cm, 27 ft·lbf}

(d) Engage the 2 fasteners.

(e) Pull back the rear seatback assembly RH.

5. CONNECT REAR CENTER SEAT OUTER BELT ASSEMBLY

(a) Connect the rear center seat outer belt assembly as shown in the illustration.

(b) Engage the 2 rear seat cushion frame hooks on the front side of the rear seat cushion assembly as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

Confirm that the rear seat cushion assembly is securely installed.

*a

Rear Seat Cushion Frame Hook Front Side

Install in this Direction

6. INSTALL REAR SEAT CENTER HEADREST ASSEMBLY

(a) Install the rear seat center headrest assembly.

7. INSTALL REAR SEAT HEADREST ASSEMBLY (for RH Side)

(a) Install the rear seat center headrest assembly.

8. INSTALL REAR SEAT HEADREST ASSEMBLY (for LH Side)

(a) Install the rear seat center headrest assembly.
