Title: 2ZR-FXE (EMISSION CONTROL): VACUUM SENSOR: REMOVAL; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

REMOVAL

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE E.F.I. VACUUM SENSOR ASSEMBLY (MANIFOLD ABSOLUTE PRESSURE SENSOR)

(a) Disconnect the E.F.I. vacuum sensor assembly (manifold absolute pressure sensor) connector.

(b) Disconnect the vacuum hose from the E.F.I. vacuum sensor assembly (manifold absolute pressure sensor).

(c) Remove the bolt and E.F.I. vacuum sensor assembly (manifold absolute pressure sensor) from the intake manifold.
