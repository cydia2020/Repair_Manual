Title: HYBRID / BATTERY CONTROL: BATTERY COOLING FILTER: REMOVAL; 2018 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2017 -        ]

REMOVAL

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE BATTERY SERVICE HOLE COVER

(a) Remove the clip.

(b) Disengage the 5 claws and remove the battery service hole cover.

2. REMOVE NO. 1 HV BATTERY INTAKE FILTER

HINT:

If the message "Maintenance requiredfor Traction battery cooling parts See owner's manual" is displayed on the multi-information display in the combination meter assembly, inspect the No. 1 HV battery intake filter.

Click here

(a) Disengage the claw.

(b) Disengage the 2 claws to remove the No. 1 HV battery intake filter from the battery service hole cover.
