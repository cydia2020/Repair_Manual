ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE NO. 1 ENGINE UNDER COVER

HINT:

Click here

2. REMOVE REAR MOTOR UNDER COVER LH

HINT:

Click here

3. INSPECT TRANSAXLE FLUID PRESSURE

NOTICE:

Make sure to perform the following inspection after inspecting and adjusting the engine.

(a) Fully apply the parking brake and chock a wheel.

(b) Disconnect No. 1 motor cooling pipe.

(1) Loosen the flare nut of the No. 1 motor cooling pipe and disconnect the No. 1 motor cooling pipe from the union.

(2) Remove the bolt and No. 1 oil cooler tube clamp from the hose bracket.

(c) Install SST to the union.

SST: 09993-19015

09993-00020

09993-00030

(d) Put the engine in maintenance mode.

HINT:

Click here

(e) Measure the hybrid transaxle fluid pressure.

Reference:

Maintenance Mode Engine Idle Speed

Hybrid Transaxle Fluid Temperature

Specified Condition

900 to 1000 rpm

20 to 50°C (68 to 122°F)

10 kPa or higher

900 to 1000 rpm

20 to 50°C (68 to 122°F)

0.1 kgf/cm 2 or higher

900 to 1000 rpm

20 to 50°C (68 to 122°F)

1.5 psi or higher

NOTICE:

Be sure to cancel maintenance mode immediately after the hybrid transaxle fluid pressure check is completed.

(f) Remove SST from the union.

(g) Connect No. 1 motor cooling pipe.

(1) Temporarily connect the No. 1 motor cooling pipe to the union with the flare nut.

(2) Install the No. 1 oil cooler tube clamp to the hose bracket with the bolt.

Torque:

11.5 N·m {117 kgf·cm, 8 ft·lbf}

(3) Using a 17 mm union nut wrench, tighten the flare nut to connect the No. 1 motor cooling pipe.

Torque:

Specified Tightening Torque :

34.3 N·m {350 kgf·cm, 25 ft·lbf}
