Title: LIGHTING (EXT): FOG LIGHT ASSEMBLY: INSPECTION; 2019 - 2022 MY Prius [12/2018 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT FOG LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH

*a

Component without harness connected

(Fog Light Assembly LH)

(a) Illumination Inspection

(1) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the fog light assembly LH and check that the light illuminates.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(W5)
Click Connector(W5)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

W5-1 (B) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

W5-2 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Fog light illuminates

If the result is not as specified, replace the fog light assembly LH.

2. INSPECT FOG LIGHT ASSEMBLY RH

*a

Component without harness connected

(Fog Light Assembly RH)

(a) Illumination Inspection

(1) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the fog light assembly RH and check that the light illuminates.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(W4)
Click Connector(W4)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

W4-1 (B) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

W4-2 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Fog light illuminates

If the result is not as specified, replace the fog light assembly RH.
