Title: HEATING / AIR CONDITIONING: HUMIDITY SENSOR: INSTALLATION; 2016 - 2020 MY Prius [11/2015 - 07/2020]

INSTALLATION

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL AIR CONDITIONING THERMISTOR TAPE (w/o Rain Sensor)

HINT:

The air conditioning thermistor tape is reusable. Only replace the tape if it is damaged or contaminated with foreign matter.

(a) Remove the air conditioning thermistor tape.

(b) Clean the air conditioning thermistor assembly sensing portion with a piece of cloth.

(c) Peel off the smaller release sheet, and then attach the air conditioning thermistor tape to the air conditioning thermistor assembly sensing portion as shown in the illustration.

HINT:

Use your fingers to push out any air bubbles from under the tape.
