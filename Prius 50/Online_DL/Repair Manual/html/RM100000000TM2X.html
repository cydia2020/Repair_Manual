Title: SEAT: FRONT SEATBACK HEATER (for Manual Seat): INSPECTION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT FRONT SEATBACK HEATER ASSEMBLY (LH SIDE)

(a) Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

Standard Resistance:

Tester Connection

Condition

Specified Condition

F-2 - F-3

Seat heatertemperature 20 °C (68 °F)

1.7 to 2.0 Ω

F-1 - F-3

Seat heatertemperature 20 °C (68 °F)

3.3 to 4.0 Ω

If the result is not as specified, replace the front seatback heater assembly (LH side).
