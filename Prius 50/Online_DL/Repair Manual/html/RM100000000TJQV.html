9. REMOVE FRONT SEAT CUSHION AIRBAG ASSEMBLY RH

CAUTION:

When storing the front seat cushion airbag assembly RH, keep the airbag deployment side facing upward.

Deployment Side

(a) Disengage the 2 clamps and claw.

HINT:

Disengage the clamps first.

(b) Remove the 3 nuts.

NOTICE:

  • The nuts must not be reused.
  • Make sure that the front seat adjuster assembly RH is not deformed. If it is, replace it with a new one.

(c) Pass the airbag wire harness through the hole to remove the front seat cushion airbag assembly RH.
