Title: SUPPLEMENTAL RESTRAINT SYSTEMS: KNEE AIRBAG ASSEMBLY: INSTALLATION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSTALLATION

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL LOWER NO. 1 INSTRUMENT PANEL AIRBAG ASSEMBLY

(a) Check that the power switch is off.

(b) Check that the cable is disconnected from the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal.

CAUTION:

Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal to disable the SRS system.

(c) Connect the airbag connector to the lower No. 1 instrument panel airbag assembly.

NOTICE:

When connecting any airbag connector, take care not to damage the airbag wire harness.

*a

Airbag Connector

*b

Airbag Connector Lock

(d) Push in the airbag connector lock to install the airbag connector.

(e) Engage the 4 claws and hook to temporarily install the lower No. 1 instrument panel airbag assembly.

(f) Install the lower No. 1 instrument panel airbag assembly with the 2 bolts.

Torque:

10 N·m {102 kgf·cm, 7 ft·lbf}

NOTICE:

Confirm that the lower No. 1 instrument panel airbag assembly is installed securely without any excessive gaps and is not protruding outward.
