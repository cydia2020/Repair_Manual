Title: 2ZR-FXE (ENGINE CONTROL): MASS AIR FLOW METER: ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT MASS AIR FLOW METER SUB-ASSEMBLY

(a) Connect the Techstream to the DLC3.

(b) Turn the power switch on (IG).

(c) Turn the Techstream on.

(d) Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Engine / Data List / Mass Air Flow Sensor.

Powertrain > Engine > Data List

Tester Display

Mass Air Flow Sensor

(e) Blow air into the mass air flow meter sub-assembly and check that the value of Mass Air Flow Sensor fluctuates.

OK:

The value changes.

If the result is not as specified, clean the mass air flow meter sub-assembly.
