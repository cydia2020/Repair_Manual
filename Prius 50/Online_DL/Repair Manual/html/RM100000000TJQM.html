CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL CURTAIN SHIELD AIRBAG ASSEMBLY

NOTICE:

When installing a curtain shield airbag assembly, have assistants hold it to prevent it from bending.

(a) Check that the power switch is off.

(b) Check that the cable is disconnected from the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal.

CAUTION:

Wait at least 90 seconds after disconnecting the cable from the negative (-) auxiliary battery terminal to disable the SRS system.

(c) Connect the airbag connector.

NOTICE:

When connecting any airbag connector, take care not to damage the airbag wire harness.

(h) Using a needle nose pliers No. 2 or equivalent, push the 6 pins into the 4 clips (A) and 2 clips (B).

NOTICE:

  • Make sure that the pins of the clips (A) and (B) are pushed in firmly.
