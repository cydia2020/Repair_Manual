15. REMOVE SHIFT LOCK CONTROL UNIT ASSEMBLY

(a) Disengage the clamp.

(b) Remove the 3 nuts and separate the shift lock control unit assembly from the No. 1 instrument panel brace sub-assembly and No. 2 instrument panel brace sub-assembly.

(c) Disconnect the shift lock control unit assembly connector to remove the shift lock control unit assembly.
