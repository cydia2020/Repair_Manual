9. REMOVE BATTERY COOLING BLOWER ASSEMBLY

(a) Disconnect the battery cooling blower assembly connector.

*A

for NICKEL METAL HYDRIDE BATTERY

*B

for LITHIUM-ION BATTERY

(b) Disengage the 2 clamps.

(c) Remove the 3 bolts.

*A

for NICKEL METAL HYDRIDE BATTERY

*B

for LITHIUM-ION BATTERY

(d) Disengage the 2 claws to remove the battery cooling blower assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Be sure not to touch the fan part of the battery cooling blower assembly.
  • Do not lift the battery cooling blower assembly using the wire harness.
