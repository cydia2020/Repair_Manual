Microphone Circuit between Microphone and Radio Receiver

DESCRIPTION

  • The radio and display receiver assembly and telephone microphone assembly*1 or map light assembly (telephone microphone assembly)*2 are connected to each other using the microphone connection detection signal lines.
  • Using this circuit, the radio and display receiver assembly sends power to the telephone microphone assembly*1 or map light assembly (telephone microphone assembly)*2, and the telephone microphone assembly*1 or map light assembly (telephone microphone assembly)*2 sends microphone signals to the radio and display receiver assembly.
  • *1: w/o Sliding Roof
  • *2: w/ Sliding Roof
