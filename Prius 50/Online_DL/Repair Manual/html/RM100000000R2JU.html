Title: CELLULAR COMMUNICATION: SAFETY CONNECT SYSTEM: B1573,B15CB; Short in Telephone Antenna Circuit; 2016 - 2017 MY Prius [11/2015 - 11/2017]

DTC

B1573

Short in Telephone Antenna Circuit

DTC

B15CB

Telematics Transceiver Antenna Disconnected

DESCRIPTION

These DTCs are stored when the DCM (Telematics Transceiver) detects an open or a short in the telephone and GPS antenna assembly circuit. The DCM (Telematics Transceiver) oscillates and receives 824 - 894 MHz or 1850 - 1990 MHz radio-frequency through the telephone and GPS antenna assembly.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

B1573

Short in Telephone Antenna Circuit

Telephone and GPS antenna assembly impedance is lower than malfunction criterion for 10 seconds when power switch is on (IG).

(Short circuit)

  • DCM (Telematics Transceiver)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna assembly
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (No. 3 antenna cord sub-assembly)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (No. 2 antenna cord sub-assembly)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (Antenna cord sub-assembly (for Antenna Side))
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (Antenna cord sub-assembly (for DCM Side))

B15CB

Telematics Transceiver Antenna Disconnected

Telephone and GPS antenna assembly impedance is higher than malfunction criterion for 10 seconds when power switch is on (IG).

(Open circuit)

  • DCM (Telematics Transceiver)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna assembly
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (No. 3 antenna cord sub-assembly)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (No. 2 antenna cord sub-assembly)
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (Antenna cord sub-assembly (for Antenna Side))
  • Telephone and GPS antenna cord (Antenna cord sub-assembly (for DCM Side))
