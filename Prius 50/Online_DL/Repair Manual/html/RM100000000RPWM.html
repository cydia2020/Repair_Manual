ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT GARAGE DOOR OPENER

(a) Press each garage door opener ("HomeLink") switch and check that the ("HomeLink") indicator light turns on. If one or more of the garage door opener ("HomeLink") switches does not turn on the ("HomeLink") indicator light, check the condition of the fuse and wire harness. if the wire of the fuse is broken or the wire harness is malfunctioning repair or replace it. If not, replace the inner rear view mirror assembly (garage door opener).
