34. REMOVE REAR COMBINATION LIGHT COVER

(a) Using a moulding remover, disengage the 4 claws as shown in the illustration.

Insert Moulding Remover Here

Remove in this Direction

(b) Disengage the 2 guides to remove the rear combination light cover as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction

35. REMOVE REAR COMBINATION LIGHT ASSEMBLY

(a) Disconnect the connector.

(b) Apply protective tape around the rear combination light assembly as shown in the illustration.

Protective Tape

(c) Remove the 2 screws.

(d) Pull the rear combination light assembly toward the rear of the vehicle as shown in the illustration to disengage the 2 pins and 2 guides and separate the rear combination light assembly.

NOTICE:

To prevent the rear combination light assembly from falling when disengaging the pins and guides, lightly hold the rear combination light assembly.
