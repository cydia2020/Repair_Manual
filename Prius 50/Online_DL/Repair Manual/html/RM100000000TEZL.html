Title: METER / GAUGE / DISPLAY: COMBINATION METER: DISASSEMBLY; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

DISASSEMBLY

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE COMBINATION METER GLASS

(a) Remove the 9 bushes.

(b) Disengage the 12 claws to remove the combination meter glass.

2. REMOVE NO. 1 COMBINATION METER COVER

(a) Remove the 2 screws.

(b) Disengage the 10 claws to remove the No. 1 combination meter cover.

3. REMOVE NO. 1 COMBINATION METER PLATE

(a) Disengage the 3 guides and remove the No. 1 combination meter plate.

4. REMOVE NO. 2 COMBINATION METER CASE

(a) Remove the screw.

(b) Disengage the 2 guides and remove the No. 2 combination meter case.

(c) Remove the 2 screws.

(d) Disengage the 2 guides and disconnect the connector to remove the No. 2 combination meter case with combination meter multi-display as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction

NOTICE:

  • Make sure to pull the connector straight up when disconnecting it as it is easily damaged.
  • Do not apply excessive force to the connector.

(e) Disengage the 2 guides to remove the combination meter multi-display from the No. 2 combination meter case as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction

5. REMOVE NO. 2 METER CIRCUIT PLATE

(a) Remove the 4 screws.

(b) Disengage the 4 guides to remove the No. 2 meter circuit plate as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction

6. REMOVE METER CIRCUIT PLATE

(a) Disengage the 4 claws and 4 guides.

(b) Disengage the 4 claws and raise the 2 holders.

Remove in this Direction

NOTICE:

Make sure not to rotate the holder 90 or more, or it may be damaged.

(c) Disconnect each connector to remove the TFT display from the meter circuit plate as shown in the illustration.
