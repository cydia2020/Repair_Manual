HOW TO DETERMINE NUT STRENGTH

HINT:

  • *: Nut with 1 or more marks on one side surface of the nut.
  • Use a nut with the same nut strength classification number (or more) as the bolt strength classification number when tightening parts with a bolt and nut.

Example:

  • Bolt = 4T
  • Nut = 4N or more
