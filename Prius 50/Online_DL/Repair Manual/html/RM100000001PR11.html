5. REMOVE ECU INTEGRATION BOX RH

(a) Disconnect each connector.

(b) Move each lock lever as shown in the illustration and disconnect the 3 hybrid vehicle control ECU connectors.

*a

Lock Lever

(c) Disengage the 3 clamps.

(d) Remove the bolt, 2 nuts and ECU integration box RH.

6. REMOVE HYBRID VEHICLE CONTROL ECU

(a) Disengage the 2 claws and remove the hybrid vehicle control ECU as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

  • If the ECU integration box is deformed or damaged, replace it.
  • Do not bend the 2 claws more than necessary.
