2. REMOVE FRONT CENTER FLOOR BRACE

(a) Remove the 4 bolts and front center floor brace.

3. REMOVE HEATED OXYGEN SENSOR

CAUTION:

To prevent burns, do not touch the engine, exhaust pipe or other high temperature components while the engine is hot.

(a) Disconnect the heated oxygen sensor connector.

*1

Wire Harness Clamp Bracket

(b) Remove the wire harness clamp.

(c) Remove the wire harness clamp bracket from the heated oxygen sensor.

(d) Using SST, remove the heated oxygen sensor from the front exhaust pipe assembly (TWC: Rear Catalyst).

SST: 09224-00011

NOTICE:

If the heated oxygen sensor has been struck or dropped, replace it.
