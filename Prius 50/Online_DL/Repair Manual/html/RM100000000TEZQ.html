Title: METER / GAUGE / DISPLAY: HEADUP DISPLAY: DISASSEMBLY; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

DISASSEMBLY

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE NO. 1 COMBINATION METER MIRROR CASE

(a) Remove the screw.

(b) Disengage the 6 claws and 2 guides to remove the No. 1 combination meter mirror case as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction

2. REMOVE NO. 1 COMBINATION METER MIRROR PLATE

(a) Remove the 5 screws and No. 1 combination meter mirror plate.

3. REMOVE COMBINATION METER MIRROR ECU

(a) Disconnect the 2 connectors.

(b) Disengage the 2 claws and raise the holder.

Remove in this Direction

NOTICE:

Make sure not to rotate the holder 90° or more, or it may be damaged.

(c) Disconnect the connector as shown in the illustration.

(d) Disengage the 2 guides to remove the combination meter mirror ECU.

4. REMOVE COMBINATION DISPLAY

(a) Remove the 2 screws.

Remove in this Direction

(b) Disengage the 2 guides to remove the combination display as shown in the illustration.

5. REMOVE COMBINATION METER WIRE

(a) Disconnect the connector.

(b) Disengage the 2 clamps to remove the combination meter wire.

6. REMOVE COMBINATION METER MIRROR

(a) Disengage the 2 claws and remove the mirror clip as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction

(b) Disengage the 2 guides to remove the combination meter mirror as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction

7. REMOVE NO. 2 COMBINATION METER MIRROR

(a) Remove the 3 screws.

(b) Disengage the 2 guides to remove the No. 2 combination meter mirror.
