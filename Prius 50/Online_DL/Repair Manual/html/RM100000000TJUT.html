INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT BACK DOOR LOCK ASSEMBLY

(a) Check the operation of the door lock motor.

(1) Move the door lock to the close (lock) position.

*a

Component without harness connected

(Back Door Lock Assembly)

*b

Close (Lock)

*c

Open (Unlock)

(2) Apply auxiliary battery voltage and check the operation of the door lock motor.
