Title: METER / GAUGE / DISPLAY: METER / GAUGE SYSTEM: B150A; Lost Communication with HMI-LAN; 2020 - 2022 MY Prius [08/2019 -        ]

DTC

B150A

Lost Communication with HMI-LAN

DESCRIPTION

The combination meter assembly (meter circuit plate) receives text data signals from the radio and display receiver assembly*1 or navigation receiver assembly*2 via local bus communication.

Based on these signals, audio and visual system*1 or navigation system*2 information is displayed on the multi-information display.

This DTC is stored when the combination meter assembly (meter circuit plate) cannot receive the signal.

DTC No.

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

Memory

Note

B150A

Lost Communication with HMI-LAN

Diagnosis Condition:

  • A registration information signal was received from the radio and display receiver assembly*1 or navigation receiver assembly*2 when ACC power source voltage was received by the radio and display receiver assembly*1 or navigation receiver assembly*2
  • The power switch is on (IG).
  • IG power source voltage is 9.5 V or more.

Malfunction Status:

  • The registration information signal is not received from the radio and display receiver assembly*1 or navigation receiver assembly*2

Malfunction Time:

  • 30 seconds or more
  • Harness or connector
  • Radio and display receiver assembly*1
  • Navigation receiver assembly*2
  • Combination meter assembly (Meter circuit plate)

DTC stored

w/ Audio Tab Display

  • *1: w/o Navigation System
  • *2: w/ Navigation System

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

When replacing the combination meter assembly, always replace it with a new one. If a combination meter assembly which was installed to another vehicle is used, the information stored in it will not match the information from the vehicle and a DTC may be stored.

HINT:

DTC B150A may be stored if the auxiliary battery voltage dropped.
