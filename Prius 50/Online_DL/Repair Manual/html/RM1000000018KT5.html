Title: CELLULAR COMMUNICATION: SAFETY CONNECT SYSTEM: B15CC; Backup Battery Failure; 2018 - 2020 MY Prius [11/2017 - 07/2020]

DTC

B15CC

Backup Battery Failure

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is set when the DCM (Telematics Transceiver) detects one of the following:

  • The BUB (Back-Up Battery) voltage drops or the BUB (Back-Up Battery) malfunctions.
  • The BUB (Back-Up Battery) temperature is (temporarily) high.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

B15CC

Backup Battery Failure

BUB (Back-Up Battery) malfunction

  • BUB (Back-Up Battery)
  • DCM (Telematics Transceiver)

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

Before performing this diagnostic procedure, make sure to perform Health Check and check that the registered DCM and VIN information is correct.

PROCEDURE

1.

CHECK DTC

(a) Turn the power switch off.

(b) Connect the Techstream to the DLC3.

(c) Turn the power switch on (IG) and wait for 10 seconds.

(d) Turn the Techstream on.

(e) Clear the DTCs.

Body Electrical > Telematics > Clear DTCs

(f) Recheck for DTCs.

Body Electrical > Telematics > Trouble Codes
