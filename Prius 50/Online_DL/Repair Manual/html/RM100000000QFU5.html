INSTALLATION

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

  • Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.
  • The following procedure is for the LH side.

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL REAR SPEAKER ASSEMBLY

(a) Engage the 2 guides to temporarily install the rear speaker assembly.

(b) Using an air riveter or a hand riveter, install the rear speaker assembly with 3 new rivets.

NOTICE:

  • Do not touch the speaker cone.
  • Do not pry on the rivets with the riveter, as this will cause damage to the riveter and mandrel.
  • Confirm that the rivets are seated properly against the speaker.
  • Do not tilt the riveter when installing the speaker with the rivets.
  • Do not leave any space between the rivet heads and speaker.
  • Do not leave any space between the speaker and door. Firmly hold the speaker and door together while installing the rivets.

HINT:

  • Install the rivets in the order shown in the illustration.
  • If the mandrel of the rivet does not come off on the first operation of the rivet gun, slide the rivet gun forward on the mandrel and operate it again.

(c) Connect the connector.
