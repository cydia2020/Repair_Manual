Title: Q510 (HYBRID TRANSMISSION / TRANSAXLE): HYBRID TRANSAXLE FLUID: ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION; 2019 - 2022 MY Prius [12/2018 -        ]

ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT HYBRID TRANSAXLE FLUID

(a) Using a 10 mm hexagon straight wrench and 10 x 12 mm offset long wrench, remove the filler plug and gasket from the rear traction motor with transaxle assembly.

*a

10 mm Hexagon Straight Wrench

*b

10 x 12 mm Offset Long Wrench

(b) Check that the hybrid transaxle fluid level is between 0 to 5 mm (0 to 0.197 in.) from the bottom lip of the filler plug opening.

NOTICE:

  • When inspecting the hybrid transaxle fluid, make sure that the vehicle is level.
  • Be sure to directly check that the hybrid transaxle fluid level is within the specified range.
  • Driving the vehicle with an insufficient or excessive amount of hybrid transaxle fluid may damage the rear traction motor with transaxle assembly.
  • If the hybrid transaxle fluid was replaced or if hybrid transaxle fluid was added, make sure to recheck the hybrid transaxle fluid level after driving the vehicle.

*a

0 to 5 mm (0 to 0.197 in.)

(c) Check for leaks if the hybrid transaxle fluid level is low. (If there are no hybrid transaxle fluid leaks but the hybrid transaxle fluid level is low, add hybrid transaxle fluid.)

Click here

(d) Using a 10 mm hexagon socket wrench, 10 mm hexagon straight wrench and 10 x 12 mm offset long wrench, install the filler plug and a new gasket to the rear traction motor with transaxle assembly.

Torque:

Specified Tightening Torque :

39 N·m {398 kgf·cm, 29 ft·lbf}

HINT:

  • Calculate the torque wrench reading when changing the fulcrum length of the torque wrench.

    Click here

  • When using a 10 x 12 mm offset long wrench (fulcrum length of 190 mm (7.48 in.)) + torque wrench (fulcrum length of 180 mm (7.09 in.)):

    19 N*m (194 kgf*cm, 14 ft.*lbf)

  • When using the 10 x 12 mm offset long wrench and torque wrench, make sure that they are parallel.

10 mm Hexagon Straight Wrench

*b

10 mm Hexagon Socket Wrench

10 x 12 mm Offset Long Wrench

*d
