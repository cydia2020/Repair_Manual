3. REMOVE FRONT SEAT INNER BELT ASSEMBLY (for Driver Side)

(a) Disconnect each connector.

(b) Disengage each clamp.

(c) for Manual Seat:

(1) Disengage the 2 clamps.

(2) Remove the nut and front seat inner belt assembly.

(d) for Power Seat:

(1) Disengage the 2 clamps.

(2) Remove the nut and front seat inner belt assembly.

(e) Remove the front seat belt anchor plate as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction

4. REMOVE FRONT SEAT INNER BELT ASSEMBLY (for Front Passenger Side)

(a) Disconnect each connector.

(b) Disengage each clamp.

(c) Disengage the clamp.

(d) Remove the nut and front seat inner belt assembly.

(e) Remove the front seat belt anchor plate as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction
