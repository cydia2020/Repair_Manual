Main Switch Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The headup display switch (No. 2 combination switch assembly) and combination meter mirror ECU are connected via direct line. The combination meter mirror ECU can be turned off and on by operating the headup display switch (No. 2 combination switch assembly).
