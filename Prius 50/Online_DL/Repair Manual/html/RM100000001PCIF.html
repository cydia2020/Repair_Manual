Title: HEATING / AIR CONDITIONING: AIR CONDITIONING SYSTEM: B1373; Lost Communication with Wiper System LIN BUS; 2021 - 2022 MY Prius [07/2020 -        ]

DTC

B1373

Lost Communication with Wiper System LIN BUS

DESCRIPTION

The air conditioning amplifier assembly communicates with the windshield wiper relay assembly, windshield wiper relay assembly and rain sensor via LIN communication.

If a malfunction occurs in the LIN communication line and a communication error occurs, this DTC is stored.
