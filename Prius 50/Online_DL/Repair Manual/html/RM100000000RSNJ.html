Title: LIGHTING (EXT): FOG LIGHT ASSEMBLY: INSPECTION; 2016 - 2018 MY Prius [11/2015 - 12/2018]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT FOG LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH

(a) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the fog light assembly LH and check that the light comes on.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(A39)
Click Connector(A39)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

A39-4 (B) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

A39-1 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Fog light comes on

A39-2 (B) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

A39-1 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Accessory light comes on

If the result is not as specified, replace the fog light assembly LH.

*a

Component without harness connected

(Fog Light Assembly LH)

2. INSPECT FOG LIGHT ASSEMBLY RH

(a) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the fog light assembly RH and check that the light comes on.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(A38)
Click Connector(A38)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

A38-4 (B) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

A38-1 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Fog light comes on

A38-2 (B) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

A38-1 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Accessory light comes on

If the result is not as specified, replace the fog light assembly RH.

*a

Component without harness connected

(Fog Light Assembly RH)
