Title: MIRROR (EXT): OUTER REAR VIEW MIRROR COVER: REMOVAL; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

REMOVAL

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

  • Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.
  • The following procedure is for the LH side.

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE OUTER MIRROR

Click here

2. REMOVE OUTER MIRROR COVER WITH OUTER MIRROR HOLE COVER

(a) Pull the outer mirror cover with outer mirror hole cover as shown in the illustration to disengage the 2 claws.

Place Hands Here

Pull In this Direction

NOTICE:

As the claws may be damaged, make sure not to apply force in any direction other than shown in the illustration.

HINT:

If it is difficult to disengage the claws, disengage them using a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape.

(b) Pull the outer mirror cover with outer mirror hole cover as shown in the illustration to disengage the 4 claws and guide.

Place Hands Here

Pull In this Direction

NOTICE:

As the claws and guide may be damaged, make sure not to apply force in any direction other than shown in the illustration.

HINT:

If it is difficult to disengage the claws, disengage them using a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape.

(c) Pull the outer mirror cover with outer mirror hole cover as shown in the illustration to disengage the 5 claws and 3 guides and remove the outer mirror cover with outer mirror hole cover.

Place Hands Here

Remove In this Direction

NOTICE:

As the claws and guides may be damaged, make sure not to apply force in any direction other than shown in the illustration.

HINT:

If it is difficult to disengage the claws, disengage them using a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape.

3. REMOVE OUTER MIRROR COVER

(a) Disengage the 2 claws to remove the outer mirror cover as shown in the illustration.
