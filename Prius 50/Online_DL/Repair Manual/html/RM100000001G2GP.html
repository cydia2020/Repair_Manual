Title: LIGHTING (EXT): REAR COMBINATION LIGHT ASSEMBLY: INSPECTION; 2019 - 2022 MY Prius [12/2018 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT TAIL LIGHT LED

(a) Illumination Inspection

(1) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the tail light LED LH and check that the lights illuminate.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(P14)
Click Connector(P14)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

P14-3(TL) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

P14-2(E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Taillight illuminates

If the result is not as specified, replace the tail light LED LH.
