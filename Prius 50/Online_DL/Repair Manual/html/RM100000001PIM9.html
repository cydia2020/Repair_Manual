Title: CRUISE CONTROL: FRONT CAMERA SYSTEM: FREEZE FRAME DATA; 2021 - 2022 MY Prius [07/2020 -        ]

FREEZE FRAME DATA

DESCRIPTION

(a) When a front camera system DTC is stored, the forward recognition camera stores the current vehicle (ECU or sensor) state as Freeze Frame Data.

CHECK FREEZE FRAME DATA

(a) Select a DTC to display the freeze frame data.

Chassis > Front Recognition Camera > Trouble Codes

(b) Check the freeze frame data for the output DTC.

Front Recognition Camera Low Temperature

Whether low temperature malfunction of forward recognition camera exists or not

Not Low Temperature or Low Temperature

Not Low Temperature: Approximately -30°C (-22°F) or higher

Low Temperature: Approximately -30°C (-22°F) or less
  • If this item is "Low Temperature", it will display that the camera is temporarily unavailable.
  • If this item is "Low Temperature", X2016 is output in the vehicle control history.

Front Recognition Camera High Temperature 1

Whether high temperature malfunction of forward recognition camera exists or not

Not High Temperature or High Temperature

Not High Temperature: Approximately 80°C (176°F) or less

High Temperature: Approximately 80°C (176°F) or higher
  • If this item is "High Temperature", the camera will be temporarily unavailable. If this does not improve even if the inside of the vehicle is left cooled for at least 10 minutes, the forward recognition camera may be malfunctioning.
  • If this item is "High Temperature", X2016 is output in the vehicle control history.

HINT:

In summertime, the windshield glass may reach 100°C (212°F) or higher

Front Recognition Camera High Temperature 2

Whether high temperature malfunction of forward recognition camera exists or not

Not High Temperature or High Temperature

Not High Temperature: Approximately 65°C (149°F) or less

High Temperature: Approximately 65°C (149°F) or higher

If this item is "High Temperature", the automatic high beam system will be temporarily unavailable. If this does not improve even if the inside of the vehicle is left cooled for at least 10 minutes, the forward recognition camera may be malfunctioning.

HINT:

In summertime, the windshield glass may reach 100°C (212°F) or higher

Heater Available Information

Whether a hot-wire heater exists in the forward recognition camera or not

Absence or Presence

Absence: w/o Heater Wire Heater

Presence: w/ Heater Wire Heater
  • The forward recognition camera checks conductivity with the hot-wire heater. If this item is "Absence", a new forward recognition camera may have been installed with the hot-wire heater disconnected.
  • If this item is "Absence", C1AAE00 is output.

Heater Control Permit

The control permission status of the hot-wire heater in the forward recognition camera

Not Allow or Allow

Not Allow: Hot-wire heater control not permitted

Allow: Hot-wire heater control permitted

If this item is "Not Allow", the following statuses are likely.

  • The forward recognition camera has stored C1AAE00
  • The forward recognition camera has stored vehicle control history
  • The air conditioning system is malfunctioning
  • Communication between the forward recognition camera and air conditioning amplifier assembly is lost

Heater Control

The control status of the hot-wire heater in the forward recognition camera

Not Control or Under Control

Not Control: Hot-wire heater not controlled

Under Control: Hot-wire heater controlled

If the hot-wire heater control permission item is "Allow" and the outside temperature is 10°C (50°F) or less, this item will repeatedly switch between "Not Control" and "Under Control". (For vehicles with whole surface deicers, this item will remain "No Control" when the whole surface deicer is in use.)
