PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL NO. 1 MOTOR COOLING HOSE

HINT:

Perform this procedure only when replacement of the motor cooling cooler is necessary.

(a) Coat a small amount of ATF as shown in the illustration.

ATF Application Area

(b) Install the No. 1 motor cooling hose to the motor cooling cooler and slide the clip to secure it.

NOTICE:

  • Be careful not to deform the motor cooling cooler.
  • Make sure to align the paint mark on the No. 1 motor cooling hose with the paint mark on the motor cooling cooler.
  • Make sure that the claws of the clip are positioned within the area shown in the illustration.

3. INSTALL MOTOR COOLING COOLER

(a) Temporarily install the motor cooling cooler to the hybrid vehicle transaxle assembly with the 3 bolts.

(b) Tighten the 3 bolts in the order shown in the illustration.

Torque:

11.5 N·m {117 kgf·cm, 8 ft·lbf}

(c) Install the No. 2 motor cooling pipe to the No. 2 motor cooling hose and slide the clip to secure it.

NOTICE:

Make sure to align the paint mark on the No. 2 motor cooling pipe with the paint mark on the No. 2 motor cooling hose.

*a

2 to 7 mm (0.0787 to 0.276 in.)

*b

Paint Mark

(d) Temporarily install the No. 2 motor cooling pipe to the hybrid vehicle transaxle assembly with the union bolt and a new gasket.

(e) Install the No. 1 oil cooler tube clamp to the hose bracket with the bolt.

Torque:

11.5 N·m {117 kgf·cm, 8 ft·lbf}

(f) Tighten the union bolt to install the No. 2 motor cooling pipe.

Torque:

35 N·m {357 kgf·cm, 26 ft·lbf}

(g) Connect the No. 1 motor cooling hose to the No. 1 motor cooling pipe and slide the clip to secure it.

NOTICE:

Make sure to align the paint mark on the No. 1 motor cooling pipe with the paint mark on the No. 1 motor cooling hose.

*a

2 to 7 mm (0.0787 to 0.276 in.)

*b

Paint Mark
