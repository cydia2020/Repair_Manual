4. REMOVE FUEL PRESSURE REGULATOR ASSEMBLY

(a) Using a screwdriver with its tip wrapped with protective tape, remove the fuel pressure regulator assembly from the fuel filter.

NOTICE:

  • Pull out the fuel pressure regulator assembly carefully because the O-rings are firmly installed between the fuel pressure regulator assembly and fuel filter.
  • Do not damage the fuel filter.
