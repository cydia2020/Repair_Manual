INSTALLATION

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL BACK DOOR OUTSIDE GARNISH SUB-ASSEMBLY WITH LIGHT

(a) Pull back the lower No. 2 back window moulding and secure it with tape as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the lower No. 2 back window moulding.

HINT:

  • Use wide tape to ensure strong enough adhesion.
  • Use non-residue tape.
  • If necessary, clean the lower No. 2 back window moulding before securing it with tape.

Lower No. 2 Back Window Moulding

Tape

(b) Install 2 new gaskets.

(c) Engage the grommet as shown in the illustration.

Install in this Direction

(d) Engage the 11 clips as shown in the illustration.

Install in this Direction

HINT:

Check that the lower No. 2 back window moulding is not caught under the back door outside garnish sub-assembly with light.

(e) Install the back door outside garnish sub-assembly with light with the 10 bolts.

Torque:

4.9 N·m {50 kgf·cm, 43 in·lbf}

(f) Remove the tape.

NOTICE:

Be careful not to damage the lower No. 2 back window moulding.

(g) Connect the connector.
