Title: DOOR / HATCH: BACK DOOR OPENER SWITCH: INSPECTION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT BACK DOOR OPENER SWITCH ASSEMBLY

(a) Check the operation of the opener switch.

(1) Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

Standard Resistance:


Click Location & Routing(R2)
Click Connector(R2)

Tester Connection

Condition

Specified Condition

R2-2 (E) - R2-3 (UL)

Back door opener switch not pushed

10 kΩ or higher

R2-2 (E) - R2-3 (UL)

Back door opener switch pushed

Below 1 Ω

If the result is not as specified, replace the back door opener switch assembly.
