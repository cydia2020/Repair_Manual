PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE SLIDING ROOF SIDE GARNISH LH

(a) Disengage the 2 claws to remove the sliding roof side garnish LH.

2. REMOVE SLIDING ROOF SIDE GARNISH RH

HINT:

Use the same procedure as for the LH side.

3. REMOVE SLIDING ROOF GLASS SUB-ASSEMBLY

(a) Move the sliding roof glass sub-assembly to the fully closed position.

(b) Using a T25 "TORX" socket wrench, remove the 4 screws and sliding roof glass sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

To prevent the sliding roof glass sub-assembly and sliding roof drive gear sub-assembly from becoming misaligned, move the sliding roof glass sub-assembly (sliding roof drive cable sub-assembly) to the fully closed position before removing.

4. REMOVE CURTAIN SHIELD AIRBAG ASSEMBLY LH

Click here

5. REMOVE CURTAIN SHIELD AIRBAG ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:

Use the same procedure as for the LH side.

6. DISCONNECT SLIDING ROOF DRAIN HOSE ASSEMBLY LH

(a) Disengage the claw and disconnect the sliding roof drain hose assembly LH.

7. DISCONNECT SLIDING ROOF DRAIN HOSE ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:

Use the same procedure as for the LH side.

8. DISCONNECT SLIDING ROOF DRAIN HOSE

HINT:

  • Use the same procedure as for the sliding roof drain hose assembly LH.
  • Use the same procedure for both sliding roof drain hoses.

9. REMOVE SLIDING ROOF HOUSING ASSEMBLY

(a) Remove the 8 bolts, 6 nuts and sliding roof housing assembly.
