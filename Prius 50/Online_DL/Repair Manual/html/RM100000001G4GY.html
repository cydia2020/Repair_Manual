Title: LIGHTING (EXT): LIGHTING SYSTEM (w/ AFS): Parking Light Circuit; 2019 - 2022 MY Prius [12/2018 -        ]

Parking Light Circuit

DESCRIPTION

When the light control switch is in the tail or head position, the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) sends an illumination request signal to the No. 1 headlight ECU sub-assembly LH to illuminate the parking lights.

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

If the No. 1 headlight ECU sub-assembly LH has been replaced, it is necessary to synchronize the vehicle information the No. 1 headlight ECU sub-assembly LH.
