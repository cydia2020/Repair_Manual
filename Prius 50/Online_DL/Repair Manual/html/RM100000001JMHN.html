Title: METER / GAUGE / DISPLAY: HEADUP DISPLAY SYSTEM: B150A; Lost Communication with HMI-LAN; 2020 - 2022 MY Prius [08/2019 -        ]

DTC

B150A

Lost Communication with HMI-LAN

DESCRIPTION

The combination meter mirror ECU receives signals from the navigation receiver assembly*1 or radio and display receiver assembly*2 via local bus communication and displays navigation system*1 and audio and visual system*2 information on the headup display.

This DTC is stored when the combination meter mirror ECU cannot receive the signal.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

Memory

B150A

Lost Communication with HMI-LAN

Diagnosis Condition:

  • A registration information signal was received from the navigation receiver assembly*1 or radio and display receiver assembly*2 when ACC power source voltage was received by the navigation receiver assembly*1 or radio and display receiver assembly*2.
  • The power switch is on (IG).
  • IG power source voltage is 9.5 V or more.

Malfunction Status:

  • The registration information signal is not received from the navigation receiver assembly*1 or radio and display receiver assembly*2.

Malfunction Time:

  • 30 seconds or more
  • Harness or connector
  • Navigation receiver assembly*1
  • Radio and display receiver assembly*2
  • Combination meter mirror ECU

DTC stored

  • *1: w/ Navigation System
  • *2: w/o Navigation System
