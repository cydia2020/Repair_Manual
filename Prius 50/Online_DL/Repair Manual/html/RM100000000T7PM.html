Title: SUPPLEMENTAL RESTRAINT SYSTEMS: AIRBAG SYSTEM: B1635/24; Rear Airbag Sensor LH Circuit Malfunction; 2016 - 2018 MY Prius [11/2015 - 12/2018]

DTC

B1635/24

Rear Airbag Sensor LH Circuit Malfunction

DESCRIPTION

The side collision sensor LH circuit consists of the airbag ECU assembly, door side airbag sensor LH, side airbag sensor assembly LH and rear airbag sensor LH.

The door side airbag sensor LH, side airbag sensor assembly LH and rear airbag sensor LH detect impacts to the vehicle and send signals to the airbag ECU assembly to determine if the airbags and pretensioners should be deployed.

DTC B1635/24 is stored when a malfunction is detected in the rear airbag sensor LH.
