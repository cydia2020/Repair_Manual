1. REMOVE REAR ROOF DRIP SIDE FINISH MOULDING SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

(a) Apply protective tape around the rear roof drip side finish moulding sub-assembly LH.

Remove in this Direction

(c) Remove the 2 clips from the rear roof drip side finish moulding sub-assembly LH.

(d) Remove the cap and gasket.
