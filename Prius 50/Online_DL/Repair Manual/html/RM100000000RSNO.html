Title: LIGHTING (EXT): REAR COMBINATION LIGHT ASSEMBLY: INSPECTION; 2016 - 2018 MY Prius [11/2015 - 12/2018]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT REAR COMBINATION LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH

(a) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the rear combination light assembly LH and check that the light comes on.
