ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT REAR WIPER MOTOR ASSEMBLY

(a) for Type A:

(1) Operate the rear wiper motor assembly.

*a

Alignment Mark

(2) Stop the rear wiper motor assembly operation.

(3) Check the automatic stop position.

HINT:

After the rear wiper motor is stopped, check the automatic stop position after lifting the wiper blade 2 times.
