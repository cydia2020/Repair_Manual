Title: LIGHTING (INT): PERSONAL LIGHT: REMOVAL; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

REMOVAL

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE ROOF CONSOLE BOX ASSEMBLY

(a) w/o Sliding Roof:

(1) Using a moulding remover, disengage the 4 clips.

(b) w/ Sliding Roof:

(1) Using a moulding remover, disengage the 4 clips.

(c) Disconnect each connector to remove the roof console box assembly.
