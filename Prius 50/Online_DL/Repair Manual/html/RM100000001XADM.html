CLEAR VEHICLE CONTROL HISTORY (HYBRID CONTROL SYSTEM)

(a) Connect the Techstream to the DLC3.

(b) Turn the power switch on (IG).

(c) Enter the following menus: Powertrain / Hybrid Control / Utility / Vehicle Control History (RoB).

NOTICE:

By performing this procedure, all stored Vehicle Control History items will be cleared.

(d) Clear the Vehicle Control History (RoB).
