Title: LIGHTING (EXT): LICENSE PLATE LIGHT ASSEMBLY: INSPECTION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT LICENSE PLATE LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH

(a) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the license plate light assembly LH and check that the light comes on.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(R4)
Click Connector(R4)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

R4-1 (B) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

R4-2 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

License plate light comes on

If the result is not as specified, replace the license plate light assembly.

*a

Component without harness connected

(License Plate Light Assembly)
