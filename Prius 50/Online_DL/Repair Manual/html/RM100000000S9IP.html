Title: P610 (HYBRID TRANSMISSION / TRANSAXLE): SHIFT LEVER KNOB: INSTALLATION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSTALLATION

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL SHIFT LEVER KNOB SUB-ASSEMBLY

(a) Turn the shift lever knob sub-assembly as shown in the illustration to install it.

(b) Using a vernier caliper, measure the height of the shift lever knob sub-assembly and adjust it as necessary.

Standard:

12.4 to 15.4 mm (0.488 to 0.606 in.)

*a

Measuring Point
