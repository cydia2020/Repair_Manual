ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT FOG FR RELAY

(a) Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

Standard Resistance:

Tester Connection

Condition

Specified Condition

3 - 5

Voltage not applied between terminals 1 and 2

10 kΩ or higher

3 - 5

Voltage applied between terminals 1 and 2

Below 1 Ω

If the result is not as specified, replace the FOG FR relay.

*a

Component without harness connected

(FOG FR Relay)
