Title: WIPER / WASHER: FRONT WIPER MOTOR: INSPECTION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSPECTION

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:

Ensure that fingers or articles of clothing do not get caught in moving parts when performing this test.

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT WINDSHIELD WIPER MOTOR ASSEMBLY

*a

Component without harness connected

(Windshield Wiper Motor Assembly)

(a) Check the LO operation.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(A24)
Click Connector(A24)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

A24-1 (+1) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

A24-5 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Motor operates at low speed (LO)

(b) Check the HI operation.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(A24)
Click Connector(A24)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

A24-4 (+2) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

A24-5 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Motor operates at high speed (HI)

If the result is not as specified, replace the windshield wiper motor assembly.
