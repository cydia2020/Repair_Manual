Title: CELLULAR COMMUNICATION: SAFETY CONNECT SYSTEM: B15C5; Manual Button Malfunction; 2016 - 2017 MY Prius [11/2015 - 11/2017]

DTC

B15C5

Manual Button Malfunction

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is set when the DCM (Telematics Transceiver) detects an open or short circuit in the manual (SOS) switch.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

B15C5

Manual Button Malfunction

Open or short circuit in manual (SOS) switch is detected.

  • DCM (Telematics Transceiver)
  • Map light assembly
  • Wire harness or connector

WIRING DIAGRAM

w/ Sliding Roof
w/o Sliding Roof
