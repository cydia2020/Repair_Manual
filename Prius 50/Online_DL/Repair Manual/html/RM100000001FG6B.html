Title: PARK ASSIST / MONITORING: BLIND SPOT MONITOR SYSTEM: Power Source Circuit; 2019 - 2022 MY Prius [12/2018 -        ]

Power Source Circuit

DESCRIPTION

This circuit provides power to operate the blind spot monitor sensor.

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:
