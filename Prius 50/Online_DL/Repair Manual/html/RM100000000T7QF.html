TC and CG Terminal Circuit

DESCRIPTION

DTC output mode is set by connecting terminals TC and CG of the DLC3.

DTCs are displayed by blinking of the SRS warning light.

HINT:

  • When multiple warning lights in the combination meter blink continuously, a short to ground in the wiring or an ECU connected to terminal TC of the DLC3 is suspected.
  • A DTC output mode signal is transmitted via CAN communication to each ECU including the airbag ECU assembly. Thus when multiple systems do not enter DTC output mode, there may be a malfunction in the hybrid vehicle control ECU.
