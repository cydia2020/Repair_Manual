Title: CRUISE CONTROL: LANE DEPARTURE ALERT SYSTEM (w/ Steering Control): OPERATION CHECK; 2016 - 2020 MY Prius [11/2015 - 07/2020]

OPERATION CHECK

CHECK LANE DEPARTURE ALERT MAIN SWITCH (STEERING PAD SWITCH ASSEMBLY)

(a) Check the lane departure alert main switch (steering pad switch assembly) on/off operation.

(1) Turn the power switch on (IG).

(2) Confirm that the lane departure alert indicator (green) in the combination meter assembly illuminates or turns off when the lane departure alert main switch (steering pad switch assembly) is pressed.

HINT:

The ON/OFF status of the lane departure alert system remains unchanged when the power switch is turned off and back to on (IG).

*a

Lane Departure Alert Indicator
