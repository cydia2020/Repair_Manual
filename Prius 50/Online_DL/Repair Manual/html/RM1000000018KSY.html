Title: CELLULAR COMMUNICATION: SAFETY CONNECT SYSTEM: B1572; Telephone Microphone Error; 2018 - 2020 MY Prius [11/2017 - 07/2020]

DTC

B1572

Telephone Microphone Error

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is set when the DCM (Telematics Transceiver) detects a malfunction in the telephone microphone assembly circuit.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

B1572

Telephone Microphone Error

Current of MCVD reaches malfunction criteria for 10 seconds while power switch is on (IG).

  • Wire harness or connector
  • Telephone microphone assembly
  • Map light assembly (w/ Sliding Roof)
  • DCM (Telematics Transceiver)
