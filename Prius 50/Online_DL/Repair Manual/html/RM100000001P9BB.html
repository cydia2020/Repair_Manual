Title: CELLULAR COMMUNICATION: SAFETY CONNECT SYSTEM (for 7 Inch Display): B15C464; Airbag Signal Signal Plausibility Failure; 2021 - 2022 MY Prius [07/2020 -        ]

DTC

B15C464

Airbag Signal Signal Plausibility Failure

DESCRIPTION

If the DCM (telematics transceiver) detects an error in communication between the DCM (telematics transceiver) and the airbag ECU assembly as a result of the DCM (telematics transceiver) self check, this DTC will be stored.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

B15C464

Airbag Signal Signal Plausibility Failure

DCM (telematics transceiver) detects an error in signals from airbag ECU assembly when power switch is on (IG).

  • Airbag system
  • Harness or connector
  • Airbag ECU assembly
  • DCM (telematics transceiver)

PROCEDURE

1.

CHECK DTC (AIRBAG SYSTEM)

(a) Turn the power switch off.

(b) Connect the Techstream to the DLC3.

(c) Turn the power switch on (IG) and wait for 10 seconds or more.

(d) Turn the Techstream on.

(e) Clear the DTCs.

Body Electrical > SRS Airbag > Clear DTCs

(f) Check for DTCs and check that no DTCs are output.

Body Electrical > SRS Airbag > Trouble Codes

No DTCs are output.
