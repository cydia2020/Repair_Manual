Title: LIGHTING (INT): DOOR COURTESY LIGHT: INSPECTION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT COURTESY LIGHT ASSEMBLY LH

(a) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the connector and check that the courtesy light comes on.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(J5)
Click Connector(J5)

Tester Connection

Condition

Specified Condition

J5-1 (B) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

J5-2 (SW) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Always

Courtesy light comes on

If the result is not as specified, replace the bulb or courtesy light assembly LH.
