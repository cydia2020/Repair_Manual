Title: LIGHTING (EXT): LIGHTING SYSTEM (w/ AFS): Power Source Circuit; 2019 - 2020 MY Prius [12/2018 - 07/2020]

Power Source Circuit

DESCRIPTION

When the power switch is on (IG) or the operation conditions of the headlights are met, the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) supplies power to the No. 1 headlight ECU sub-assemblies via the No. 1 integration relay.

When the power switch is on (IG), the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) supplies power to the No. 1 headlight ECU sub-assemblies via the IG1-NO. 1 relay.
