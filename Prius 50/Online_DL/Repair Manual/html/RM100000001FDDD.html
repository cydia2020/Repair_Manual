Title: LIGHTING (EXT): LIGHTING SYSTEM (w/ AFS): B2414; Steering Position Sensor; 2019 - 2022 MY Prius [12/2018 -        ]

DTC

B2414

Steering Position Sensor

DESCRIPTION

The No. 1 headlight ECU sub-assembly LH receives signals indicating direction and amount the steering is turned from the steering sensor using CAN communication. The No. 1 headlight ECU sub-assembly LH stores DTC B2414.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

Note

B2414

Steering Position Sensor

Malfunction in steering sensor

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

  • The lighting system uses the CAN communication system. Inspect the communication functions by following How to Proceed with Troubleshooting. Troubleshoot the lighting system after confirming that the communication systems are functioning properly.

  • If the No. 1 headlight ECU sub-assembly LH has been replaced, it is necessary to synchronize the vehicle information and initialize the No. 1 headlight ECU sub-assembly LH.
