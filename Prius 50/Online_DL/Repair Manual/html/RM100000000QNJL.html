Title: 2ZR-FXE (LUBRICATION): OIL PUMP: REASSEMBLY; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

REASSEMBLY

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL OIL PUMP COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY

(a) Coat the oil pump drive rotor and oil pump driven rotor with engine oil, and place them into the oil pump body with the marks facing the oil pump cover sub-assembly side.

*a

Mark

(b) Install the oil pump cover sub-assembly to the oil pump body with the 5 bolts.

Torque:

8.8 N·m {90 kgf·cm, 78 in·lbf}

2. INSTALL OIL PUMP RELIEF VALVE

(a) Coat the oil pump relief valve with engine oil.

*1

Oil Pump Relief Valve Plug

*2

Oil Pump Relief Valve Spring

*3

Oil Pump Relief Valve

(b) Insert the oil pump relief valve and oil pump relief valve spring into the oil pump body.

(c) Using a 27 mm socket wrench, install the oil pump relief valve plug to the oil pump body.

Torque:

49 N·m {500 kgf·cm, 36 ft·lbf}
