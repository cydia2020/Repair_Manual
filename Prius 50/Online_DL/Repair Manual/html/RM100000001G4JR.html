Title: STEERING COLUMN: HEATED STEERING WHEEL CONTROLLER: INSTALLATION; 2019 - 2022 MY Prius [12/2018 -        ]

INSTALLATION

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

  • Do not replace the spiral cable with sensor sub-assembly with the auxiliary battery connected and the power switch on (IG).
  • Do not rotate the spiral cable with sensor sub-assembly without the steering wheel assembly installed with the auxiliary battery connected and the power switch on (IG).
  • Ensure that the steering wheel assembly is installed and aligned straight when inspecting the steering sensor.

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL STEERING WHEEL HEATER CONTROL ASSEMBLY

(a) Install the steering wheel heater control assembly to the lower steering wheel boss cover with the 2 screws.

(b) Connect the connector to the steering wheel heater control assembly.

(c) Engage the clamp.

(d) Engage the 6 claws and 4 pins to install the lower steering wheel boss cover to the steering wheel assembly.
