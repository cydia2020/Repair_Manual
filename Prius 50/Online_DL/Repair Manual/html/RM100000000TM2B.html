(d) Apply auxiliary battery voltage and check the operation of the front seat heater according to the table below.

NOTICE:

To prevent the front seat heater from overheating, be sure to disconnect the auxiliary battery from the front seat heater immediately after this inspection is completed.

OK:

Auxiliary battery Connection

Specified Condition

Auxiliary battery positive (+) → Terminal b5-1*1

Auxiliary battery positive (+) → Terminal M82-6*2

  • Front seat cushion heater temperature 30 to 50°C (86 to 122°F)
  • Front seatback heater temperature 30 to 50°C (86 to 122°F)

Auxiliary battery negative (-) → Terminal b5-2 and b5-3*1

Auxiliary battery negative (-) → Terminal M82-7 and M82-5*2

  • *1: for Power Seat
  • *2: for Manual Seat
