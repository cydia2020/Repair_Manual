Title: INTERIOR PANELS / TRIM: UPPER INSTRUMENT PANEL: DISASSEMBLY; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

DISASSEMBLY

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE METER MIRROR SUB-ASSEMBLY (w/ Headup Display)

Click here

2. REMOVE NO. 1 INSTRUMENT PANEL PIN

(a) Remove the 4 screws <A> and 4 No. 1 instrument panel pins.

3. REMOVE DEFROSTER NOZZLE ASSEMBLY

(a) Remove the 3 screws <A>.

(b) Disengage the 9 guides to remove the defroster nozzle assembly.

4. REMOVE AUTOMATIC LIGHT CONTROL SENSOR

Click here

5. REMOVE NAVIGATION ANTENNA ASSEMBLY WITH BRACKET (w/ Navigation Antenna)

Click here

6. REMOVE ANTENNA CORD SUB-ASSEMBLY (w/ Navigation Antenna)

Click here

7. REMOVE SIDE DEFROSTER NOZZLE ASSEMBLY LH

(a) Remove the 2 screws <A>.

(b) Disengage the 2 guides to remove the side defroster nozzle assembly LH.

8. REMOVE SIDE DEFROSTER NOZZLE LH

(a) Disengage the 3 claws to remove the side defroster nozzle LH.

9. REMOVE SIDE DEFROSTER NOZZLE ASSEMBLY RH

(a) Remove the 2 screws <A>.

(b) Disengage the 2 guides to remove the side defroster nozzle assembly RH.

10. REMOVE SIDE DEFROSTER NOZZLE RH

(a) Disengage the 3 claws to remove the side defroster nozzle RH.

11. REMOVE NO. 2 INSTRUMENT PANEL WIRE

(a) Disengage the 10 clamps to remove the No. 2 instrument panel wire.

12. REMOVE INSTRUMENT PANEL CUSHION

(a) Remove the instrument panel cushion.
