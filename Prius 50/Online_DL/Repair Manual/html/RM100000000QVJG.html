3. FULLY TIGHTEN FRONT LOWER NO. 1 SUSPENSION ARM SUB-ASSEMBLY RH

(a) Using SST, fully tighten the bolt (A).

*a

Torque Wrench Fulcrum Length

Turn

SST: 09961-01270

Torque:

Specified tightening torque :

240 Nm {2447 kgfcm, 177 ftlbf}

HINT:

  • Calculate the torque wrench reading when changing the fulcrum length of the torque wrench.

    Click here

  • When using SST (fulcrum length of 200 mm (7.87 in.)) + torque wrench (fulcrum length of 600 mm (1.97 ft.)):

    180 N*m (1835 kgf*cm, 133 ft.*lbf)

(b) Fully tighten the bolt (B).

Torque:

125 Nm {1275 kgfcm, 92 ftlbf}

NOTICE:

Because the nut has its own stopper, do not turn the nut. Tighten the bolt with the nut secured.
