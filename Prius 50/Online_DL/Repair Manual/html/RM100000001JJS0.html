Title: SUPPLEMENTAL RESTRAINT SYSTEMS: AIRBAG SYSTEM: B1638; Curtain Shield Airbag Sensor Initialization Error (LH); 2020 - 2022 MY Prius [08/2019 -        ]

DTC

B1638

Curtain Shield Airbag Sensor Initialization Error (LH)

DESCRIPTION

The side collision sensor LH circuit (bus 1) consists of the airbag ECU assembly, door side airbag sensor LH and rear airbag sensor LH.

The door side airbag sensor LH and rear airbag sensor LH detect impacts to the vehicle and send signals to the airbag ECU assembly to determine if the airbags and pretensioners should be deployed.

DTC B1638 is stored when a malfunction is detected in the side collision sensor LH circuit (bus 1).
