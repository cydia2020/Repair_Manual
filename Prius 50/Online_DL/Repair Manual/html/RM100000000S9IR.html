9. REMOVE MOTOR COOLING COOLER

(a) Slide the clip and disconnect the No. 1 motor cooling hose from the No. 1 motor cooling pipe.

(b) Remove the bolt and No. 1 oil cooler tube clamp from the hose bracket.

(c) Remove the union bolt and gasket to disconnect the No. 2 motor cooling pipe from the hybrid vehicle transaxle assembly.

(d) Slide the clip and remove the No. 2 motor cooling pipe from the No. 2 motor cooling hose.

(e) Remove the 3 bolts and motor cooling cooler from the hybrid vehicle transaxle assembly.

NOTICE:

Make sure not to remove the No. 1 motor cooling hose and No. 2 motor cooling hose from the motor cooling cooler as the motor cooling cooler may be deformed.
