Title: 2ZR-FXE (COOLING): WATER PUMP: INSTALLATION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSTALLATION

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL ENGINE WATER PUMP ASSEMBLY

(a) Install a new gasket to the engine water pump assembly.

(b) Move the engine water pump assembly toward the rear of the vehicle.

Install in this Direction

Protective Tape

(c) Move the engine water pump assembly toward the top of the vehicle and set the engine water pump assembly to the installation location.

Install in this Direction

Protective Tape

(d) Temporarily install the engine water pump assembly with the 5 bolts.

Bolt Length:

Item

Length

Bolt (A)

55 mm (2.17 in.)

Bolt (B)

30 mm (1.18 in.)

(e) Operate the jack and install the engine assembly with transaxle to the vehicle.

CAUTION:

  • Be careful not to get your hand or fingers pinched when raising or lowering the engine assembly with transaxle.
  • Do not raise the engine assembly with transaxle more than necessary. If the engine is raised excessively, the vehicle may also be lifted up.

NOTICE:

When raising or lowering the engine assembly with transaxle, be careful of other components.

(f) Connect the engine mounting insulator sub-assembly RH to the engine mounting bracket RH with the 2 bolts.

Torque:

72 N·m {734 kgf·cm, 53 ft·lbf}

(g) Install the nut.

Torque:

41 N·m {418 kgf·cm, 30 ft·lbf}

(h) Tighten the 4 bolts.

Torque:

Bolt (A) :

25.5 N·m {260 kgf·cm, 19 ft·lbf}

Bolt (B) :

21 N·m {214 kgf·cm, 15 ft·lbf}

(i) Using SST, tighten the bolt.

SST: 09961-00950

Torque:

25.5 N·m {260 kgf·cm, 19 ft·lbf}

HINT:

  • Calculate the torque wrench reading when changing the fulcrum length of the torque wrench.

    Click here

  • When using SST (fulcrum length of 150 mm (5.91 in.)) + torque wrench (fulcrum length of 180 mm (7.09 in.)): 14 N*m (143 kgf*cm, 10 ft.*lbf)

*a

Torque Wrench Fulcrum Length

(j) Connect the engine water pump assembly connector.

(k) Connect the 2 cooler brackets of the air conditioner tube and accessory assembly with the bolt and nut.

Torque:

Bolt :

9.8 N·m {100 kgf·cm, 87 in·lbf}

Nut :

5.4 N·m {55 kgf·cm, 48 in·lbf}
