Title: 2ZR-FXE (LUBRICATION): OIL PRESSURE SWITCH: REMOVAL; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

REMOVAL

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE ENGINE OIL PRESSURE SWITCH ASSEMBLY

(a) Disconnect the engine oil pressure switch assembly connector.

(b) Using a 24 mm deep socket wrench, remove the engine oil pressure switch assembly.
