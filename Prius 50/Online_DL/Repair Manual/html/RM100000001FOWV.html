Title: LIGHTING (EXT): HEADLIGHT ASSEMBLY: DISASSEMBLY; 2019 - 2022 MY Prius [12/2018 -        ]

DISASSEMBLY

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

  • Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.
  • The following procedure is for the LH side.

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE NO. 1 HEADLIGHT ECU SUB-ASSEMBLY (w/ AFS)

Click here

2. REMOVE HEADLIGHT GASKET (w/ AFS)

Click here

3. REMOVE NO. 1 HEADLIGHT COVER (w/ AFS)

(a) Turn the No. 1 headlight cover as shown in the illustration to remove it.

Remove in this Direction

4. REMOVE CLEARANCE LIGHT BULB (for Bulb Type Clearance Light)

(a) Disconnect the connector.

(b) Turn the clearance light socket with the clearance light bulb as shown in the illustration to remove them as a unit.

Remove in this Direction

(c) Remove the clearance light bulb from the clearance light socket.

5. REMOVE CLEARANCE LIGHT SOCKET (for Bulb Type Clearance Light)

6. REMOVE HEADLIGHT FRONT TURN SIGNAL LED

(a) Disconnect the connector.

(b) Turn the headlight front turn signal LED as shown in the illustration to remove it.

Remove in this Direction

7. REMOVE FRONT SIDE MARKER LIGHT BULB

(a) Disconnect the connector.

(b) Turn the front marker light socket and wire with the front side marker light bulb as shown in the illustration to remove them as a unit.

Remove in this Direction

(c) Remove the front side marker light bulb from the front marker light socket and wire.

8. REMOVE FRONT MARKER LIGHT SOCKET AND WIRE

9. REMOVE HEADLIGHT UNIT ASSEMBLY
