Title: LIGHTING (INT): LUGGAGE COMPARTMENT ROOM LIGHT BULB: REPLACEMENT; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

REPLACEMENT

PROCEDURE

1. SEPARATE NO. 1 LUGGAGE COMPARTMENT LIGHT ASSEMBLY

(a) Using a moulding remover, disengage the claw to separate the No. 1 luggage compartment light assembly.

2. REMOVE LUGGAGE COMPARTMENT LIGHT BULB

(a) Disengage the claw as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction

(b) Disengage the 2 claws and remove the shade from the No. 1 luggage compartment light lens as indicated by the arrows, in the order as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction (1)

Remove in this Direction (2)

(c) Remove the luggage compartment light bulb from the No. 1 luggage compartment light lens as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction

3. INSTALL LUGGAGE COMPARTMENT LIGHT BULB

(a) Install the luggage compartment light bulb to the No. 1 luggage compartment light lens as shown in the illustration.

Install in this Direction

(b) Engage the 3 claws to install the shade to the No. 1 luggage compartment light lens as shown in the illustration.

Install in this Direction

4. INSTALL NO. 1 LUGGAGE COMPARTMENT LIGHT ASSEMBLY

(a) Engage the claw to install the No. 1 luggage compartment light assembly.
