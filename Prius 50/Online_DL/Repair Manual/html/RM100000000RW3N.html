IG Power Source Circuit

DESCRIPTION

Power source voltage is supplied to the air conditioning amplifier assembly when the power switch is turned on (IG). This power is used for operating the air conditioning amplifier assembly, servo motors, etc.

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following procedure.
