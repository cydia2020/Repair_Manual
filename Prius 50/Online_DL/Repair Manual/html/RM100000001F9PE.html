DTC

B1500

Fuel Sender Open Detected

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when the combination meter assembly (meter circuit plate) detects a fuel sender gauge assembly malfunction via a direct line.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

Memory

Note

B1500

Fuel Sender Open Detected

When IG voltage is 9.5 V or more and the following condition is detected:

  • Voltage signal from fuel sender gauge assembly is 0.2 V or less 3 times consecutively
  • Voltage signal from fuel sender gauge assembly is 4.7 V or more 3 times consecutively
  • Harness or connector
  • Fuel suction tube with pump and gauge assembly
  • Fuel sender gauge assembly
  • Combination meter assembly (Meter circuit plate)
