Title: LIGHTING (EXT): LIGHTING SYSTEM (w/o AFS): Back-up Light Circuit; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

Back-up Light Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The hybrid vehicle control ECU controls the back-up lights via the BKUP LP relay.

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

  • Inspect the fuses for circuits related to this system before performing the following inspection procedure.
  • Before replacing the hybrid vehicle control ECU, refer to Registration.
