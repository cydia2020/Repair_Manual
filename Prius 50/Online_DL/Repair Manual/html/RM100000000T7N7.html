Title: HYBRID / BATTERY CONTROL: HYBRID BATTERY SYSTEM (for LITHIUM-ION BATTERY): P1A6017,P31AA17; Hybrid/EV Battery Stack 2 Cell Circuit Voltage Above Threshold; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

DTC

P1A6017

Hybrid/EV Battery Stack 2 Cell Circuit Voltage Above Threshold

DTC

P31AA17

Hybrid/EV Battery Stack 1 Cell Circuit Voltage Above Threshold

DESCRIPTION

The HV battery is composed of 56 cells (3.6 V each) in series. The battery ECU assembly monitors the voltage of each HV battery cell to detect malfunctions of the HV battery.

MONITOR DESCRIPTION

If the battery ECU assembly detects that the voltage of a HV battery cell is excessively high, the battery ECU assembly will illuminate the MIL and store a DTC.

CONFIRMATION DRIVING PATTERN

HINT:

  • After repair has been completed, clear the DTC and then check that the vehicle has returned to normal by performing the following All Readiness check procedure.

    Click here

  • When clearing the permanent DTCs, refer to the "CLEAR PERMANENT DTC" procedure.

    Click here

  1. Connect the Techstream to the DLC3.
  2. Turn the power switch on (IG) and turn the Techstream on.
  3. Clear the DTCs (even if no DTCs are stored, perform the clear DTC procedure).
  4. Turn the power switch off and wait for 2 minutes or more.
  5. Turn the power switch on (IG) and turn the Techstream on.
  6. Apply the parking brake and secure the wheels using chocks.[*1]
  7. Turn the power switch on (READY), drive (D) selected and the vehicle stopped, firmly depress the brake pedal and accelerator pedal. The engine rpm will increase as well as the value of Data List item "Hybrid Battery SOC". If the value drops, release the accelerator pedal and then depress it again to continue charging.[*2]
  8. Check that charging stops when the Data List item "Hybrid Battery SOC" reaches the upper limit.[*3]

    HINT:

    [*1] to [*3]: Normal judgment procedure.

    The normal judgment procedure is used to complete DTC judgment and also used when clearing permanent DTCs.

  9. Enter the following menus: Powertrain / HV Battery / Utility / All Readiness.
  10. Check the DTC judgment result.

    HINT:

    • If the judgment result shows NORMAL, the system is normal.
    • If the judgment result shows ABNORMAL, the system has a malfunction.
    • If the judgment result shows INCOMPLETE or N/A, perform driving pattern again.

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:

  • When disposing of an HV battery, make sure to return it through an authorized collection agent who is capable of handling it safely. If the HV battery is returned via the manufacturer specified route, it will be returned properly and in a safe manner by an authorized collection agent.
