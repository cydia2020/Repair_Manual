Title: LIGHTING (INT): GLOVE BOX LIGHT: INSPECTION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT GLOVE BOX LIGHT ASSEMBLY

(a) Inspect the glove box light.

(1) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to each terminal of glove box light assembly connector and check the operation of the glove box light.

Result:


Click Location & Routing(F11)
Click Connector(F11)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

F11-2 (B) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

F11-1 (L) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

Glove box light comes on

If the result is not as specified, replace the glove box light assembly.
