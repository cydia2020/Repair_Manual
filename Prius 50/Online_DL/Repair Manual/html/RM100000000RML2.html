1. REMOVE REAR SUSPENSION MEMBER SUB-ASSEMBLY

2. REMOVE REAR UPPER CONTROL ARM ASSEMBLY LH

(a) Remove the bolt, nut and rear upper control arm assembly LH from the rear suspension member sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

Because the nut has its own stopper, do not turn the nut. Loosen the bolt with the nut secured.

3. REMOVE REAR UPPER CONTROL ARM ASSEMBLY RH

HINT:

Perform the same procedure as for the LH side.
