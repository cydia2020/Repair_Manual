Title: MIRROR (EXT): OUTER REAR VIEW MIRROR GLASS: INSTALLATION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSTALLATION

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

  • Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.
  • The following procedure is for the LH side.

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL OUTER MIRROR

(a) w/o Blind Spot Monitor System:

(1) Connect the connector.

(b) w/ Blind Spot Monitor System:

(1) Engage the clamp.

Install in this Direction

(2) Engage the claw as shown in the illustration.

(3) Connect the 2 connectors.

(c) Engage the 4 claws to install the outer mirror.

NOTICE:

Do not push the outer mirror with excessive force. Doing so may break the mirror surface.
