Title: LIGHTING (EXT): LED ILLUMINATION BEAM: INSPECTION; 2019 - 2022 MY Prius [12/2018 -        ]

INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT LED ILLUMINATION LIGHT LH

*a

Component without harness connected

(LED Illumination Light LH)

(a) Illumination Inspection

(1) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the LED illumination light LH and check that the light illuminates.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(W7)
Click Connector(W7)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

W7-2 (B) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

W7-1 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

LED illumination light illuminates

If the result is not as specified, replace the LED illumination light LH.

2. INSPECT LED ILLUMINATION LIGHT RH

*a

Component without harness connected

(LED Illumination Light RH)

(a) Illumination Inspection

(1) Apply auxiliary battery voltage to the LED illumination light RH and check that the light illuminates.

OK:


Click Location & Routing(W6)
Click Connector(W6)

Tester Connection

Specified Condition

W6-2 (B) - Auxiliary battery positive (+)

W6-1 (E) - Auxiliary battery negative (-)

LED illumination light illuminates

If the result is not as specified, replace the LED illumination light RH.
