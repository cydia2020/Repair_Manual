INSTALLATION

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL HV INVERTER COOLING FAN

CAUTION:

Be sure to wear insulated gloves.

HINT:

Perform this procedure only when replacement of the HV inverter cooling fan is necessary.

(a) Install the 3 collars to the bushes of the HV inverter cooling fan.

HINT:

  • Be sure to install the collar in the correct orientation.

    *1

    collar

    *a

    bush

  • If any of the bushes have come off, reinstall the bushes to the HV inverter cooling fan before installing the collars.

(b) Using a T20 "TORX" socket wrench, install the HV inverter cooling fan with 3 bolts..

Torque:

1.5 N·m {15 kgf·cm, 13 in·lbf}

HINT:

When replacing the installation bolts:

  • The shape of supply part bolts will differ.
  • The supply part bolts do not come with washers, so be sure to prepare washers when replacing them.
  • When tightening the supply bolts, use a plus socket wrench.

(c) Engage the 2 guides.

(d) Engage the clamp.

(e) Connect the hybrid motor control inverter assembly connector.

2. INSTALL HV INVERTER COOLING COVER

CAUTION:

Be sure to wear insulated gloves.

HINT:

Perform this procedure only when replacement of the HV inverter cooling fan is necessary.

(a) Using a T20 "TORX" socket wrench, install the HV inverter cooling cover with 4 bolts.

Torque:

1.5 N·m {15 kgf·cm, 13 in·lbf}

HINT:

When replacing the installation bolts:

  • The shape of supply part bolts will differ.
  • The supply part bolts do not come with washers, so be sure to prepare washers when replacing them.
  • When tightening the supply bolts, use a plus socket wrench.

3. INSTALL REAR HYBRID MOTOR CONTROL INVERTER ASSEMBLY

CAUTION:

Be sure to wear insulated gloves.

(a) Temporarily install the rear hybrid motor control inverter assembly with 3 bolts.

NOTICE:

  • Do not damage the terminals, connector housing or rear hybrid motor control inverter assembly during disconnection.
  • Cover the hole where the cable was connected with tape (non-residue type) or equivalent to prevent entry of foreign matter.
  • Do not allow any foreign matter or water to enter the rear hybrid motor control inverter assembly.
  • Insulate the disconnected terminals with insulating tape.
  • To prevent damage due to staticelectricity, do not touch the terminals of the disconnected connectors.

(b) Fully tighten the 3 bolts as shown in the illustration.

Torque:

12 N·m {122 kgf·cm, 9 ft·lbf}

(c) Connect the 2 rear hybrid motor control inverter assembly connectors.

NOTICE:

To prevent damage due to staticelectricity, do not touch the terminalsof the disconnected connectors.
