Title: LIGHTING (INT): LUGGAGE COMPARTMENT ROOM LIGHT: REMOVAL; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

REMOVAL

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE NO. 1 LUGGAGE COMPARTMENT LIGHT ASSEMBLY

(a) Using a moulding remover, disengage the claw.

(b) Disconnect the connector to remove the No. 1 luggage compartment light assembly.

2. REMOVE LUGGAGE COMPARTMENT LIGHT BULB

(a) Disengage the claw as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction

(b) Disengage the 2 claws and remove the shade from the No. 1 luggage compartment light lens as indicated by the arrows, in the order as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction (1)

Remove in this Direction (2)

(c) Remove the luggage compartment light bulb from the No. 1 luggage compartment light lens as shown in the illustration.

Remove in this Direction
