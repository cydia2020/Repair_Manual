DTC

B1350

Speaker Circuit

DESCRIPTION

The vehicle approaching speaker assembly circuit consists of the vehicle approaching speaker controller and vehicle approaching speaker assembly.

This DTC is stored when a malfunction is detected in the vehicle approaching speaker assembly circuit or vehicle approaching speaker controller.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

B1350

Speaker Circuit

Detection condition:

  • The power switch is on (IG).
  • IG power source voltage is 10 V or more.

Malfunction status:

  • When ECU output sound signal is being monitored with warning sound not being output, a speaker circuit open, short or short to ground is detected.
  • When vehicle proximity warning sound output conditions are met, an internal malfunction is continuously detected in the vehicle approaching speaker controller.

Malfunction duration:

  • 6 seconds or more
  • 3 seconds or more
  • Vehicle approaching speaker controller
  • Vehicle approaching speaker assembly
  • Harness or connector
