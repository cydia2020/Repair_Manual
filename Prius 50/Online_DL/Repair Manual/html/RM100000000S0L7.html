Title: DOOR / HATCH: BACK DOOR SUPPORT: DISPOSAL; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

DISPOSAL

PROCEDURE

1. DISPOSE OF BACK DOOR STAY ASSEMBLY

(a) Secure the back door stay assembly horizontally in a vise with the piston rod pulled out.

(b) While wearing safety glasses, gradually cut the shaded part between A and B shown in the illustration using a metal saw to gradually release the gas.

Specification:

Area

Measurement

A-B

50 mm (1.97 in.)

CAUTION:

Although the gas inside the back door stay assembly is colorless, odorless and harmless, metal debris may scatter. Therefore, cover the back door stay assembly with a piece of cloth or other material.
