Drive Start Control System

DESCRIPTION

The drive start control is controlled by the hybrid vehicle control ECU.

If the hybrid vehicle control ECU determines that the shift lever and accelerator pedal are operated abnormally, driving torque is restricted and, when necessary, a warning is displayed on the combination meter assembly.

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

HINT:

Even if the accelerator pedal position is maintained, the system output may increase when driving uphill and decrease when driving downhill. This is due to drive start control controlling the driving torque, and is not a malfunction.

2.

PAST ACTIVATION CONFIRMATION

(a) Check if the customer operated the vehicle in a way that would cause the drive start control to operate.

Drive Start Control Activation Conditions

HINT:

Condition 1 (Reverse control)

Activation conditions

  • Reverse (R) is selected.
  • The accelerator pedal is depressed.

Items Controlled

  • Driving torque is restricted.

Deactivation Conditions

  • The shift state is changed to any state other than reverse (R).
  • The accelerator pedal is released.

Condition 2 (Shift control A)

Activation conditions

  • The shift state is changed from park (P) to any forward state (drive (D) or brake (B)).
  • The accelerator pedal is depressed approximately 1/5 or more.
    • If the shift lever is moved to attempt to change the shift state from park (P) when the accelerator pedal is depressed, the shift state will not change and drive-start control will not operate.

Items Controlled

  • Driving torque is restricted.

Deactivation Conditions

  • Shift state is changed to park (P) or neutral (N).
  • The accelerator pedal is released.

Condition 3 (Shift control B)

Activation conditions

  • The shift state is changed from reverse (R) to any forward state (drive (D) or brake (B)), any forward state (drive (D) or brake (B)) to reverse (R), or Neutral (N) to reverse (R).
  • The accelerator pedal is depressed approximately 1/5 or more.

Items Controlled

  • Driving torque is restricted.

Deactivation Conditions

  • Shift state is changed to park (P) or neutral (N).
  • The accelerator pedal is released.

HINT:

During reverse control or shift control, driving torque is adjusted based on the road gradient and steering angle.
