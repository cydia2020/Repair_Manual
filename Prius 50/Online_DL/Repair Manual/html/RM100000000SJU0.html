Title: HEATING / AIR CONDITIONING: CONDENSER: REASSEMBLY; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

REASSEMBLY

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL COOLER DRYER

(a) Using pliers, install a new cooler dryer to the modulator.

*a

Modulator

(b) Sufficiently apply compressor oil to 3 O-rings and the fitting surfaces of a new cap.

Compressor Oil:

ND-OIL 11 or equivalent

NOTICE:

  • Keep the O-rings and O-ring fitting surfaces free from dirt or foreign matter.
  • Do not use any compressor oil other than ND-OIL 11 or equivalent.

(c) Using a 14 mm straight hexagon socket wrench, install the cap to the modulator.

Torque:

4.0 N·m {41 kgf·cm, 35 in·lbf}

*a

Modulator
