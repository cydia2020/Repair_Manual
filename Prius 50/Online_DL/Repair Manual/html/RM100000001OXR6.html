Title: SEAT: POWER SEAT TRACK RAIL ASSEMBLY: INSTALLATION; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

INSTALLATION

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

CAUTION:

Wear protective gloves. Sharp areas on the parts may injure your hands.

PROCEDURE

1. INSTALL UPPER SEAT TRACK RAIL SUB-ASSEMBLY LH

CAUTION:

Make sure to read the warnings printed on the box of the upper seat track rail sub-assembly LH before performing operations.

NOTICE:

  • In order to reduce the risk of damage to the part and to ensure ease of operations, secure a space of 80 mm (3.15 in.) or more as shown in the illustration.

    *a

    80 mm (3.15 in.)

  • Secure enough space to ensure that the upper seat track rail sub-assembly LH does not contact any other objects during installation.

(a) Install a new upper seat track rail sub-assembly LH with the 8 nuts.

Torque:

25 N·m {255 kgf·cm, 18 ft·lbf}

(b) Engage the clamp.

(c) Engage the clamp and claw to install the front seat lower No. 1 cushion shield LH as shown in the illustration.

Install in this direction

(d) Engage the guide and claw to install the front seat lower cushion shield LH as shown in the illustration.

Install in this direction

(e) Engage the guide and claw to install the front seat lower cushion shield RH as shown in the illustration.

Clamp

*b

Guide

Install in this direction

(f) Engage the clamp.

(g) Engage the guide and 2 claws as shown in the illustration.

Install in this direction

(h) Install the 2 screws to install the front seat cushion wire harness holder LH.

(i) Engage the claw and 2 clamps.

(j) Connect the slide motor connector.
