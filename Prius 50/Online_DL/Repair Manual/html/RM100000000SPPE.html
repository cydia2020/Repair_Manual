Title: 2ZR-FXE (ENGINE CONTROL): CAMSHAFT OIL CONTROL VALVE: REMOVAL; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

REMOVAL

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE NO. 1 ENGINE COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY

Click here

2. REMOVE CAMSHAFT TIMING OIL CONTROL VALVE ASSEMBLY

(a) Disconnect the camshaft timing oil control valve assembly connector.

(b) Remove the bolt and camshaft timing oil control valve assembly from the cylinder head cover sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Do not allow foreign matter to contact the oil seal surface of the camshaft timing oil control valve assembly (connecting surface with the cylinder head cover sub-assembly).
  • If the camshaft timing oil control valve assembly has been struck or dropped, replace it.

(c) Remove the O-ring from the camshaft timing oil control valve assembly.
