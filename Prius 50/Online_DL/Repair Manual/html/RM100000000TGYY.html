Title: NETWORKING: LIN COMMUNICATION SYSTEM: B1206; P/W Master Switch Communication Stop; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

DTC

B1206

P/W Master Switch Communication Stop

DESCRIPTION

This DTC is stored when LIN communication between the multiplex network master switch assembly and main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) stops for 10 seconds or more.

DTC No.

Detection Item

DTC Detection Condition

Trouble Area

B1206

P/W Master Switch Communication Stop

No communication between multiplex network master switch assembly and main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) for 10 seconds or more.

  • Multiplex network master switch assembly
  • Main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU)
  • Instrument panel junction block assembly
  • Wire harness or connector
  • ECU-B NO. 2 fuse

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT
