Daytime Running Light Circuit

DESCRIPTION

When the operation conditions of the daytime running lights are met, the main body ECU (multiplex network body ECU) sends an illumination request signal to the No. 1 headlight ECU sub-assembly and illuminates the daytime running lights.

WIRING DIAGRAM

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

If the No. 1 headlight ECU sub-assembly LH has been replaced, it is necessary to synchronize the vehicle information the No. 1 headlight ECU sub-assembly LH.
