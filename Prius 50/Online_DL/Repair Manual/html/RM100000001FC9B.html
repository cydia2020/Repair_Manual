6. REMOVE REAR DRIVE SHAFT ASSEMBLY

(a) Put matchmarks on the rear drive shaft assembly and differential side gear shaft sub-assembly.

*a

Matchmark

(b) Remove the 4 nuts and 4 washers.

(c) Put matchmarks on the rear drive shaft assembly and rear axle hub and bearing assembly.

*a

Matchmark

(d) Using a plastic hammer, separate the rear drive shaft assembly from the rear axle hub and bearing assembly.

HINT:

If it is difficult to remove, tap the end of the rear drive shaft assembly using a brass bar and a hammer.

(e) Push the rear drive shaft inboard joint assembly toward the outside of the vehicle and remove the rear drive shaft assembly from the differential side gear shaft sub-assembly.

NOTICE:

  • Do not damage the rear drive shaft inboard joint boot.
  • Do not damage the rear drive shaft outboard joint boot.
  • Do not drop the rear drive shaft assembly.
  • When removing the rear drive shaft assembly, keep it level.
