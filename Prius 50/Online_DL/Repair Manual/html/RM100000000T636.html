5. REMOVE NAVIGATION RECEIVER ASSEMBLY WITH BRACKET

(a) Remove the 4 bolts.

(b) Pull the navigation receiver assembly with bracket toward the rear of the vehicle and disengage the 2 guides.

Remove in this Direction

(c) Disconnect each connector and remove the navigation receiver assembly with bracket.

6. DISCONNECT STEREO COMPONENT TUNER ASSEMBLY WITH WIRE (w/ Satellite Radio)

(a) Disconnect the 3 connectors to disconnect the stereo component tuner assembly with wire.

7. REMOVE NO. 1 RADIO BRACKET

(a) w/o Satellite Radio:

(1) Remove the 3 screws and No. 1 radio bracket.

(b) w/ Satellite Radio:

(1) Remove the 5 screws and No. 1 radio bracket.

8. REMOVE NO. 2 RADIO BRACKET

(a) w/o Satellite Radio:

(1) Remove the 3 screws and No. 2 radio bracket.

(1) Remove the 5 screws and No. 2 radio bracket.
