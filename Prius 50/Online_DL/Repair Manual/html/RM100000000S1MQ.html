Title: WIPER / WASHER: FRONT WIPER RUBBER: REPLACEMENT; 2016 - 2022 MY Prius [11/2015 -        ]

REPLACEMENT

CAUTION / NOTICE / HINT

NOTICE:

Make sure to hold the front wiper arm while lifting it as lifting the front wiper arm by the front wiper blade may damage or deform the front wiper blade.

HINT:

  • Use the same procedure for the RH side and LH side.
  • The following procedure is for the LH side.

PROCEDURE

1. REMOVE FRONT WIPER BLADE

(a) Push the lock button of the front wiper blade to release the lock.

(b) Remove the front wiper blade from the front wiper arm as shown in the illustration.

NOTICE:

Place a piece of cloth between the front wiper arm and windshield glass to avoid damaging the windshield glass.

2. REMOVE WIPER RUBBER

HINT:

The wiper rubber can be removed from either end of the front wiper blade.

(a) Gradually slide the wiper rubber until the end of the wiper rubber is aligned with the slits in the front wiper blade as shown in the illustration.
