ON-VEHICLE INSPECTION

PROCEDURE

1. INSPECT WINDSHIELD WIPER MOTOR ASSEMBLY

(a) for LH Side:

(1) Operate the windshield wiper motor assembly.

*a

Alignment Mark

(2) Stop the windshield wiper motor assembly operation.

(3) Check the automatic stop (park) position.

HINT:

After the front wiper motor is stopped, check the automatic stop position after lifting the wiper blade 2 times.

Standard Clearance:

Area

Measurement

A

7.5 mm (0.295 in.)

OK:
